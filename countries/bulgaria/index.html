🇧🇬 Bulgaria Schengen Visa Guide 2026

Updated: February 1, 2026 · 15 min read

BIG NEWS: Bulgaria officially joined the Schengen Area on March 31, 2024 (air/sea borders) and January 1, 2025 (land borders)! This is a HUGE opportunity because Bulgaria has the LOWEST financial requirements in all of Schengen - just €50 per day. Plus, their embassies are less crowded = easier appointments and potentially better approval rates!

Required Documents

  1. Completed visa application form
    • Download from Bulgarian embassy website
    • Fill in English or Bulgarian
    • Sign and date
  2. Valid passport
    • Valid 3+ months after return date
    • Issued within last 10 years
    • 2+ blank pages
    • Copy of all used pages
  3. Two passport photos (35x45mm, white background, recent)
  4. Travel insurance (€30,000 coverage minimum, valid all Schengen)
  5. Flight reservation (round-trip, don't buy until approved!)
  6. Accommodation proof
    • Hotel bookings for entire stay
    • OR invitation letter if staying with someone
  7. Bank statements (last 3-6 months, stamped by bank)
  8. Employment proof
    • Employment letter on company letterhead
    • Last 3 months salary slips
    • Leave approval letter
  9. Cover letter explaining your trip
  10. Travel itinerary (day-by-day plan)

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Documents (1-2 weeks)

  • Get bank statements stamped
  • Request employment letter from HR
  • Purchase travel insurance
  • Book hotels with free cancellation
  • Get flight reservation (not ticket!)

Step 2: Book Appointment (2-4 weeks ahead)

  • Visit Bulgarian embassy website for your country
  • Book visa appointment online or by phone
  • Choose morning slots if possible (officers are fresher)

Step 3: Attend Appointment

  • Arrive 15 minutes early
  • Bring ALL original documents + photocopies
  • Pay visa fee: €90 (adults), €45 (children 6-12)
  • Submit biometrics (photo, fingerprints)
  • Answer interview questions honestly

Step 4: Wait for Decision (10-15 days)

  • Standard processing: 10-15 working days
  • Can extend to 30-45 days if extra verification needed
  • Track online if tracking number provided

Step 5: Collect Passport

  • Pick up in person or by courier (check embassy rules)
  • Check visa dates immediately!
  • Verify your name spelling is correct

Insider Tips for Bulgaria Visa Approval

🎯 Unique Advantages of Applying to Bulgaria:
  • Lower rejection rates currently: As a new member, Bulgaria is welcoming tourists - their current approval rate is around 85%
  • Easier appointments: Unlike France/Germany embassies with 2-month waits, Bulgaria often has slots within 1-2 weeks
  • Budget-friendly angle works: Bulgaria understands budget travelers - €50/day requirement reflects this
  • Less scrutiny on "why Bulgaria": You can honestly say you're visiting for Black Sea beaches, mountains, Sofia - they're happy to promote tourism

What to Emphasize in Your Application

  1. Genuine interest in Bulgaria:
    • Mention specific places: Sunny Beach, Nessebar, Rila Monastery, Plovdiv Old Town
    • Show you've researched Bulgarian culture and attractions
    • Don't just say "passing through" - make Bulgaria your destination
  2. Financial stability (even with low requirements):
    • Show regular income deposits in bank statements
    • Avoid sudden large deposits right before applying
    • €70-80/day demonstrates comfort, not just minimum compliance
  3. Strong ties to home country:
    • Stable employment (1+ years at current job is ideal)
    • Family ties (spouse, children, parents)
    • Property ownership or long-term rental agreements
    • Previous travel history showing you returned from past trips

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Applying to Bulgaria just because it's "easier" without showing genuine interest in visiting
  • ❌ Booking non-refundable flights before visa approval
  • ❌ Showing exactly €50/day - go slightly higher (€60-70)
  • ❌ Vague itinerary like "sightseeing" - be specific about places
  • ❌ Missing bank stamps on statements (they MUST be stamped!)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit other Schengen countries with a Bulgaria visa?

YES! A Bulgaria Schengen visa lets you visit all 29 Schengen countries. You can fly into Sofia, explore Bulgaria for a few days, then visit Greece, Italy, France - anywhere in Schengen. Just make sure Bulgaria is either your main destination (most days) or your first entry point.

Is Bulgaria really in Schengen now?

Yes! Bulgaria joined on March 31, 2024 for air and sea borders, and January 1, 2025 for land borders. This means no passport checks when traveling from Bulgaria to other Schengen countries by air, sea, or land.

Why is Bulgaria's requirement so low (€50/day)?

Bulgaria has a lower cost of living than Western Europe, so they set realistic requirements. €50/day is actually sufficient for a comfortable trip in Bulgaria - hotels, meals, transport, and activities are much cheaper than France or Spain.

Can I apply for Bulgaria visa if I'm mainly visiting other countries?

You should apply to the country where you spend most nights. If you're spending 7 nights in France and 3 in Bulgaria, apply to France. BUT if you're doing 5 nights in Bulgaria and 5 in Greece, you can apply to Bulgaria (your first entry point). Don't try to game the system - they verify your itinerary!

How long does Bulgaria visa processing take?

Standard is 10-15 working days. Can extend to 30-45 days if they need additional verification. Apply at least 3-4 weeks before your trip to be safe.

Do I need to speak Bulgarian?

No! Visa applications can be submitted in English. In tourist areas, English is widely spoken. Your cover letter and itinerary should be in English (or Bulgarian if you can, but not required).
