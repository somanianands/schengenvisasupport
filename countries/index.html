Common Questions

Can I visit other countries with a France visa?

Yep! A Schengen visa from any country lets you visit all 29 Schengen countries. So a visa from France works in Spain, Germany, Italy, Bulgaria, Romania - everywhere.

Which country is easiest to get a Schengen visa from?

Honestly? It depends on your profile. But generally, Netherlands and Germany have efficient processes and good approval rates. Spain is typically stricter with financial requirements.

What if I'm visiting 5 countries equally?

Apply to whichever country you're flying into first. That's your entry point, and that's who should process your visa.
