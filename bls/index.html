Frequently Asked Questions

Which countries does BLS process visas for?

BLS International primarily processes Spain Schengen visas worldwide. They also handle Portugal visas in select locations and Italy in limited centers. Check your country-specific BLS portal for exact services offered.

Is BLS the same as VFS Global?

No, they are different visa service providers. BLS primarily handles Spain, while VFS handles most other Schengen countries. Both provide similar services (biometrics, document submission) but operate different centers.

Does BLS accept cash payments?

NO. All BLS centers worldwide operate cashless. Only card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) or online payments accepted.

How long does Spain visa processing take via BLS?

Standard processing: 10-15 working days after Spanish embassy receives your documents. BLS forwards applications within 1-2 days. Peak season (summer) may take 15-20 days. Total timeline: 2-4 weeks from appointment.

Can I apply for other Schengen countries at BLS?

BLS primarily handles Spain. For other Schengen countries (France, Germany, Italy, etc.), you typically need to apply via VFS Global or embassy directly. Check your country-specific availability on BLS website.
