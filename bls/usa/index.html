Frequently Asked Questions

Which BLS center should I apply from in USA?

Apply from the BLS center that serves your state. Check regional coverage above. You must apply from the designated center for your state of residence.

Can US citizens apply for Spain visa via BLS?

US citizens don't need visa for short stays (tourism, business) up to 90 days in Spain/Schengen area. BLS is for non-US residents (visa nationals) living in USA who need Spain visa.

Does BLS USA accept cash?

NO. All BLS centers in USA are 100% cashless. Only credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) accepted. NO cash, NO checks.

What are BLS USA operating hours?

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (application submission). Saturday, Sunday, federal holidays: CLOSED. Arrive 15 minutes before appointment time.

How long does Spain visa take from USA?

Standard processing: 10-15 working days after Spanish consulate receives your documents from BLS. Total timeline including BLS processing: 2-3 weeks from appointment date.
