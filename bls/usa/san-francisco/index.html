🇪🇸 BLS Spain Visa San Francisco - Complete Guide 2026

Updated: February 12, 2026

Need a Spain Schengen visa from San Francisco? BLS International San Francisco is your official visa application center for Spain. Serving the Pacific Northwest and West Coast (N.CA, NV, OR, WA, ID, MT, WY, AK), this center handles Spain Schengen visa applications with appointment-based services and cashless operations.

💳 IMPORTANT: NO CASH ACCEPTED

BLS San Francisco is 100% cashless. Only card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) accepted. Carry your debit/credit card for all payments.

How to Reach BLS San Francisco

🚇 By BART (Recommended)

  • Multiple BART lines: Powell St, Montgomery St, Embarcadero stations
  • Walk 5-10 minutes from station to BLS center
  • Check bart.gov for routes and schedules
  • BART is most reliable transit in Bay Area

🚊 By Muni Metro/Bus

  • Muni Metro lines (J, K, L, M, N, T) serve downtown
  • Multiple Muni bus routes available
  • Check sfmta.com for routes and real-time arrivals

🚗 By Car/Rideshare

  • Uber/Lyft available throughout Bay Area
  • Limited street parking (metered, expensive)
  • Public parking garages available ($25-45/day)
  • Recommend BART/Muni for convenience

Regional Coverage

BLS San Francisco serves:

  • Northern California

US residents from these states must apply at BLS San Francisco for Spain Schengen visas.

Application Process

  1. Book Appointment: Visit blsspain-usa.com, create account, book slot (2-3 weeks advance)
  2. Prepare Documents: Passport, US visa/Green Card, photos, insurance ($50K coverage), bank statements (3 months), tickets, hotels, employment letter
  3. Pay Fees: Online via website or card at center (NO CASH)
  4. Attend Appointment: Submit documents, biometrics (fingerprints, photo)
  5. Track Status: Via blsspain-usa.com using reference number
  6. Collect Passport: Self-pickup or courier delivery ($25-$40)

Processing Time

  • Standard: 10-15 working days after Spanish consulate receives documents
  • Peak Season (May-Sep): 15-20 working days
  • BLS Forwarding: 1-2 days to Spanish consulate
  • Total Time: 2-4 weeks from appointment

Tips for Success

  • ✅ Book appointment 2-3 weeks in advance
  • ✅ Arrive 15 minutes before scheduled time
  • ✅ Carry original documents + 1 set of photocopies
  • ✅ Bring valid US visa or Green Card
  • ✅ Bring debit/credit card for payment
  • ✅ Dress neatly for biometrics photo
  • ✅ Use BART/Muni for easiest access
  • ❌ NO cash accepted - only cards
  • ❌ NO walk-ins without appointment
