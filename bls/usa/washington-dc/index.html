🇪🇸 BLS Spain Visa Washington DC - Complete Guide 2026

Updated: February 12, 2026

Need a Spain Schengen visa from Washington DC? BLS International Washington DC is your official visa application center for Spain. Serving the Mid-Atlantic and Southeast region (DC, MD, VA, WV, DE, NC, SC), this center handles Spain Schengen visa applications with appointment-based services and cashless operations.

💳 IMPORTANT: NO CASH ACCEPTED

BLS Washington DC is 100% cashless. Only card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) accepted. Carry your debit/credit card for all payments.

How to Reach BLS Washington DC

🚇 By Metro (Recommended)

  • Red Line: Serves downtown via Metro Center, Gallery Place
  • Blue/Orange/Silver Lines: Farragut West, McPherson Square
  • Walk 5-15 minutes from nearest station to BLS center
  • Metro is most convenient for downtown DC access

🚌 By Bus

  • Metrobus serves downtown extensively
  • Check WMATA website for routes and schedules
  • Circulator buses also available in downtown

🚗 By Car/Taxi/Rideshare

  • Uber/Lyft available throughout DC area
  • Limited street parking (metered)
  • Public parking garages available ($20-40/day)
  • Consider Metro for easier access

Regional Coverage

BLS Washington DC serves:

  • District of Columbia
  • Maryland, Virginia, West Virginia
  • Delaware
  • North Carolina, South Carolina

US residents from these states/territories must apply at BLS Washington DC for Spain Schengen visas.

Application Process

  1. Book Appointment: Visit blsspain-usa.com, create account, book slot (2-3 weeks advance)
  2. Prepare Documents: Passport, US visa/Green Card, photos, insurance ($50K coverage), bank statements (3 months), tickets, hotels, employment letter
  3. Pay Fees: Online via website or card at center (NO CASH)
  4. Attend Appointment: Submit documents, biometrics (fingerprints, photo)
  5. Track Status: Via blsspain-usa.com using reference number
  6. Collect Passport: Self-pickup or courier delivery ($25-$40)

Processing Time

  • Standard: 10-15 working days after Spanish consulate receives documents
  • Peak Season (May-Sep): 15-20 working days
  • BLS Forwarding: 1-2 days to Spanish consulate
  • Total Time: 2-4 weeks from appointment

Tips for Success

  • ✅ Book appointment 2-3 weeks in advance
  • ✅ Arrive 15 minutes before scheduled time
  • ✅ Carry original documents + 1 set of photocopies
  • ✅ Bring valid US visa or Green Card
  • ✅ Bring debit/credit card for payment
  • ✅ Dress neatly for biometrics photo
  • ✅ Use Metro for easiest access
  • ❌ NO cash accepted - only cards
  • ❌ NO walk-ins without appointment
