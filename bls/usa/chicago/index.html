🇪🇸 BLS Spain Visa Chicago - Complete Guide 2026

Updated: February 12, 2026

Need a Spain Schengen visa from Chicago? BLS International Chicago is your official visa application center for Spain. This center serves Illinois, Indiana, Wisconsin, Michigan, Ohio, Minnesota, Iowa, Missouri, North Dakota, South Dakota, Nebraska, and Kansas residents.

💳 IMPORTANT: NO CASH ACCEPTED

BLS Chicago is 100% cashless. Only card payments accepted. Carry your debit/credit card for all payments.

📍 How to Reach BLS Chicago

By CTA Train (Recommended)

The Chicago BLS center is easily accessible via the CTA 'L' train system:

  • Red Line: Alight at State/Lake station
  • Blue Line: Alight at Washington station, transfer or walk to State/Lake
  • Brown Line: Alight at State/Lake station
  • Walking: 2-5 minute walk from station to State Street

Tip: Use Ventra Card for seamless CTA travel. The center is in the heart of the Loop.

By Car

If driving, note that downtown Chicago parking is limited and expensive. Nearby parking garages charge $20-35/day. Public transit is strongly recommended.

📋 Required Documents

  1. Valid US Passport (2 blank pages, valid 3+ months beyond return)
  2. Completed Visa Application Form
  3. Two Passport Photos (recent, Schengen specification)
  4. Travel Insurance (€30,000 minimum coverage)
  5. Flight Bookings (round-trip reservation)
  6. Hotel Bookings (entire trip duration)
  7. Bank Statements (last 3 months)
  8. Employment Letter (on company letterhead)
  9. Cover Letter (explaining trip purpose)
  10. Previous Visas (if applicable)

⏰ Processing Time

Standard Processing: 15-30 days from submission

Peak Season: May-August applications may take longer

Recommendation: Apply 4-6 weeks before travel

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does BLS Chicago accept cash payments?

No. BLS Chicago is 100% cashless. Only card payments accepted.

Can I submit documents for my family members?

Children under 12 don't need to appear personally. Adults and children 12+ must appear for biometrics (unless provided within 59 months).

What if my application is rejected?

You can reapply immediately with improved documentation. There's no waiting period.

Can I travel to other Schengen countries with Spain visa?

Yes, Spain visa allows travel across all 29 Schengen countries. Spain must be your main destination.

Is interview required at BLS Chicago?

Generally, no interview. You submit documents and biometrics. Embassy may call for interview in rare cases.
