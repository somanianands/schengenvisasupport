Updated: February 12, 2026

💳 IMPORTANT: NO CASH ACCEPTED

BLS New York is 100% cashless. Only card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) accepted. Carry your debit/credit card for all payments.

US residents from these states must apply at BLS New York for Spain Schengen visas.

Application Process

  1. Book Appointment: Visit blsspain-usa.com, create account, book slot (2-3 weeks advance)
  2. Prepare Documents: Passport, US visa/Green Card, photos, insurance ($50K coverage), bank statements (3 months), tickets, hotels, employment letter
  3. Pay Fees: Online via website or card at center (NO CASH)
  4. Attend Appointment: Submit documents, biometrics (fingerprints, photo)
  5. Track Status: Via blsspain-usa.com using reference number
  6. Collect Passport: Self-pickup or courier delivery ($25-$40)

Processing Time

  • Standard: 10-15 working days after Spanish consulate receives documents
  • Peak Season (May-Sep): 15-20 working days
  • BLS Forwarding: 1-2 days to Spanish consulate
  • Total Time: 2-4 weeks from appointment

Tips for Success
