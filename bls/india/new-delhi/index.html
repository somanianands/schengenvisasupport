Frequently Asked Questions

Where is BLS Spain Visa Center located in New Delhi?

BLS International New Delhi is located in the Connaught Place area, Central Delhi. Easily accessible via Delhi Metro Blue Line (Rajiv Chowk) and Yellow Line. Serves Delhi NCR, Haryana, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, J&K, Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, and Chandigarh.

Does BLS New Delhi accept cash payments?

NO. BLS New Delhi operates 100% cashless. Only debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex) accepted. NO cash, NO demand drafts, NO cheques.

What are Spain visa fees at BLS New Delhi?

Adult (18+): ₹7,100 visa fee + ₹2,500-₹3,500 BLS service fee. Child (6-17): ₹3,550 + service fee. Child (0-5): Free. Optional courier: ₹600-₹900. Total adult: ₹9,600-₹11,500.

What are BLS New Delhi operating hours?

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM (application submission and document collection). Saturday, Sunday, public holidays: CLOSED. Arrive 15 minutes before appointment.

Which regions does BLS New Delhi serve?

BLS New Delhi serves: Delhi, NCR (Gurgaon, Noida, Ghaziabad, Faridabad), Haryana, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, and Chandigarh Union Territory.

How to reach BLS New Delhi by Metro?

Take Delhi Metro Blue Line or Yellow Line to Rajiv Chowk station (Connaught Place). Exit toward Connaught Place. BLS center is within 5-10 minutes walk. Alternatively, Patel Chowk (Yellow Line) or Barakhamba Road (Blue Line) stations are also nearby.

Is parking available at BLS New Delhi?

Limited paid parking available in Connaught Place area. Recommended to use Metro/public transport due to heavy traffic and parking constraints in CP. Commercial parking lots available nearby at ₹40-₹100/hour.

How far in advance should I book BLS New Delhi appointment?

Book 15-30 days before intended travel. Peak season (April-July, December): appointments fill 4-6 weeks ahead. Off-season: 2-3 weeks availability. Check blsspainvisa.com/india daily for slots.

What documents needed for Spain visa at BLS New Delhi?

Required: Valid passport (10-year validity, 3 months beyond return), completed application form, 2 photos (35x45mm), travel insurance (₹30 lakh/€30,000), flight bookings, hotel reservations, bank statements (6 months, ₹50,000+ balance), leave approval, ITR, Spain itinerary, cover letter.

Can I walk-in without appointment at BLS New Delhi?

NO. Walk-ins not accepted. All applicants MUST book online appointment via blsspainvisa.com/india. Without confirmed appointment, entry denied.

Can someone else submit my application at BLS New Delhi?

NO. Personal appearance MANDATORY for all applicants (including children) for biometric fingerprints and photo. Only exception: applicants who gave biometrics for Schengen visa within last 59 months may submit via authorized representative with proper authorization letter.

How long does Spain visa processing take from BLS New Delhi?

Standard processing: 10-15 working days after Spanish Embassy receives documents from BLS (1-2 days forwarding time). Peak season may extend to 15-20 days. Total timeline: 2-3 weeks from appointment date. Plan travel 4-6 weeks ahead.

How to track Spain visa application from BLS New Delhi?

Log in to blsspainvisa.com/india with application reference number and date of birth. Check status: Application Submitted → Under Process → Decision Made → Ready for Collection. SMS alerts available (₹100 optional fee).

Can I reschedule BLS New Delhi appointment?

Yes. Log in to blsspainvisa.com/india, go to your appointment, select 'Reschedule'. Must reschedule at least 2 working days before original appointment. One free reschedule allowed; additional changes may incur ₹500 fee.

How to collect passport from BLS New Delhi?

In-person collection during 9 AM-4 PM (Mon-Fri) with receipt and ID, OR courier delivery (₹600-₹900 optional charge). Track online when status shows 'Ready for Collection'. Bring original receipt and valid ID proof.
