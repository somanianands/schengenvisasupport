🇪🇸 BLS Spain Visa Hyderabad - Complete Guide 2026

Updated: February 12, 2026

Need a Spain Schengen visa from Hyderabad? BLS International Hyderabad is your official visa application center for Spain. Serving Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, this center handles Spain Schengen visa applications with appointment-based services and cashless operations.

💳 IMPORTANT: NO CASH ACCEPTED

BLS Hyderabad is 100% cashless. Only card payments (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex) accepted. Carry your debit/credit card for all payments.

Quick Facts: BLS Hyderabad

Location Banjara Hills/Jubilee Hills area, Hyderabad
Metro Access Red Line - HITEC City station
Serves Regions Telangana, Andhra Pradesh
Timings Mon-Fri: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Adult Visa Fee ₹7,100 + ₹2,500-₹3,500 service fee
Phone +91-11-46524646 (national helpline)
Website india.blsspainvisa.com
Payment Card only - NO CASH

How to Reach BLS Hyderabad

🚇 By Hyderabad Metro (Recommended)

  • Red Line: Miyapur → HITEC City station
  • Exit metro station, take auto-rickshaw or app cab to BLS center
  • Auto-rickshaws available (₹80-120 from HITEC City metro)
  • Ola/Uber widely available in Hyderabad

🚌 By TSRTC Bus

  • Multiple TSRTC routes to Banjara Hills/Jubilee Hills
  • Key routes serve major tech hubs and residential areas
  • Alight at nearest stop to BLS center

🚗 By Car/Taxi

  • Uber/Ola/Rapido widely available
  • Limited parking - arrive early or use cabs
  • Two-wheeler parking may be available
  • Commercial parking nearby (₹30-50/hour)

Regional Coverage

BLS Hyderabad serves:

  • Telangana (entire state) - All districts
  • Andhra Pradesh (entire state) - All districts

Residents from these two states must apply at BLS Hyderabad for Spain Schengen visas.

Application Process

  1. Book Appointment: Visit india.blsspainvisa.com, create account, book slot (2-3 weeks advance)
  2. Prepare Documents: Passport, photos, insurance (€50K), bank statements (6 months), tickets, hotels, employment letter
  3. Pay Fees: Online via website or card at center (NO CASH)
  4. Attend Appointment: Submit documents, biometrics (fingerprints, photo)
  5. Track Status: Via blsspainvisa.com using reference number
  6. Collect Passport: Self-pickup or courier delivery (₹600-₹900)

Processing Time

  • Standard: 10-15 working days after embassy receives documents
  • Peak Season (May-Aug): 15-20 working days
  • BLS Forwarding: 1-2 days to Spanish embassy
  • Total Time: 2-4 weeks from appointment

Tips for Success

  • ✅ Book appointment 2-3 weeks in advance
  • ✅ Arrive 15 minutes before scheduled time
  • ✅ Carry original documents + 1 set of photocopies
  • ✅ Bring debit/credit card for payment
  • ✅ Dress neatly for biometrics photo
  • ✅ Keep mobile phone handy for OTP/verification
  • ✅ Check document checklist on blsspainvisa.com
  • ❌ NO cash accepted - only cards
  • ❌ NO walk-ins without appointment
