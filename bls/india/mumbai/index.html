Frequently Asked Questions

Where is BLS Spain Visa Center located in Mumbai?

BLS International Mumbai is located in BKC (Bandra Kurla Complex) or Andheri area. Accessible via Western Line local trains and Mumbai Metro. Serves Maharashtra (except Pune district), Goa, Madhya Pradesh, and Chhattisgarh.

Does BLS Mumbai accept cash payments?

NO. BLS Mumbai operates 100% cashless. Only debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex) accepted. NO cash, NO demand drafts, NO cheques.

What are Spain visa fees at BLS Mumbai?

Adult (18+): ₹7,100 visa fee + ₹2,500-₹3,500 BLS service fee. Child (6-17): ₹3,550 + service fee. Child (0-5): Free. Optional courier: ₹600-₹900. Total adult: ₹9,600-₹11,500.

What are BLS Mumbai operating hours?

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM (application submission and document collection). Saturday, Sunday, public holidays: CLOSED. Arrive 15 minutes before appointment.

Which regions does BLS Mumbai serve?

BLS Mumbai serves: Maharashtra (all districts except Pune), Goa, Madhya Pradesh, and Chhattisgarh. Pune district residents must apply at BLS Pune.

How to reach BLS Mumbai by local train?

Take Western Line to BKC or Andheri station. From BKC: 10-minute walk or auto/taxi. From Andheri: Metro/auto available. Central Line passengers: change at Dadar to Western Line.

Is parking available at BLS Mumbai?

Limited paid parking available at BKC commercial buildings. BKC parking: ₹50-₹100/hour. Heavy traffic in BKC/Andheri - public transport (local train/Metro) strongly recommended.

How far in advance should I book BLS Mumbai appointment?

Book 15-30 days before intended travel. Peak season (April-July, December): appointments fill 4-6 weeks ahead. Off-season: 2-3 weeks availability. Check blsspainvisa.com/india regularly for slots.

What documents needed for Spain visa at BLS Mumbai?

Required: Valid passport (10-year validity, 3 months beyond return), completed application form, 2 photos (35x45mm), travel insurance (₹30 lakh/€30,000), flight bookings, hotel reservations, bank statements (6 months, ₹50,000+ balance), leave approval, ITR, Spain itinerary, cover letter.

Can I walk-in without appointment at BLS Mumbai?

NO. Walk-ins not accepted. All applicants MUST book online appointment via blsspainvisa.com/india. Without confirmed appointment, entry denied.

Can someone else submit my application at BLS Mumbai?

NO. Personal appearance MANDATORY for all applicants (including children) for biometric fingerprints and photo. Only exception: applicants who gave biometrics for Schengen visa within last 59 months may submit via authorized representative.

How long does Spain visa processing take from BLS Mumbai?

Standard processing: 10-15 working days after Spanish Consulate receives documents from BLS (1-2 days forwarding time). Peak season may extend to 15-20 days. Total timeline: 2-3 weeks from appointment date.

How to track Spain visa application from BLS Mumbai?

Log in to blsspainvisa.com/india with application reference number and date of birth. Check status: Application Submitted → Under Process → Decision Made → Ready for Collection. SMS alerts available (₹100 optional).

Can I reschedule BLS Mumbai appointment?

Yes. Log in to blsspainvisa.com/india, go to your appointment, select 'Reschedule'. Must reschedule at least 2 working days before original appointment. One free reschedule allowed; additional changes may incur ₹500 fee.

How to collect passport from BLS Mumbai?

In-person collection during 9 AM-4 PM (Mon-Fri) with receipt and ID, OR courier delivery to Mumbai address (₹600-₹900 optional). Track online when status shows 'Ready for Collection'. Bring original receipt and valid ID proof.
