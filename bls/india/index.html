Frequently Asked Questions

Which BLS center should I apply from?

Apply from the BLS center that serves your state/region. Check regional coverage above or city-specific pages. You must apply from the designated center for your region.

Can I visit any BLS center or must I go to a specific one?

You MUST visit the BLS center designated for your state/region of residence. Each center has jurisdictional coverage. Check your region in the coverage list above.

Does BLS India accept cash?

NO. All BLS centers in India are 100% cashless. Only debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex) accepted. NO cash, NO demand drafts.

What are BLS India operating hours?

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM (application submission). Saturday, Sunday, public holidays: CLOSED. Arrive 15 minutes before appointment time.

How long does Spain visa take from India?

Standard processing: 10-15 working days after Spanish embassy receives your documents from BLS. Total timeline including BLS processing: 2-3 weeks from appointment date.
