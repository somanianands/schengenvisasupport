🇪🇸 BLS Spain Visa Edinburgh - Complete Guide 2026

Updated: February 12, 2026

Need a Spain Schengen visa from Edinburgh? BLS International Edinburgh is your official visa application centre for Spain. This centre serves all of Scotland including Edinburgh, Glasgow, Aberdeen, Dundee, Inverness, the Highlands, and the Islands.

💳 IMPORTANT: NO CASH ACCEPTED

BLS Edinburgh is 100% cashless. Only card payments accepted. Carry your debit/credit card for all payments.

Quick Facts: BLS Edinburgh

Location 32 Princes Street, Central Edinburgh, Edinburgh EH1 1SR
Public Transport Edinburgh Trams to Princes Street; Lothian Buses to Princes Street stops
Serves Regions All of Scotland
Timings Mon-Fri: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Adult Visa Fee £72 (embassy) + £35-55 (BLS service fee)
Phone +44-131-555-0100
Website blsspain-uk.com
Payment Card only - NO CASH

📍 How to Reach BLS Edinburgh

By Tram (Recommended)

The Edinburgh BLS centre is easily accessible via Edinburgh Trams:

  • Edinburgh Trams: Alight at Princes Street stop
  • Walking: The centre is directly on Princes Street

By Bus

Multiple Lothian Buses routes serve Princes Street:

  • Routes: 3, 5, 7, 8, 14, 19, 29, 31, 33, 37, 47, 49 (and many more)
  • Alight at any Princes Street stop

Tip: Use contactless payment or Ridacard for convenient bus/tram travel.

By Car

If driving, limited parking available at NCP car parks (£15-25/day). Trams and buses are recommended for easy city centre access.

📋 Required Documents

  1. Valid UK Passport or BRP (2 blank pages, valid 3+ months beyond return)
  2. Completed Visa Application Form
  3. Two Passport Photos (recent, Schengen specification)
  4. Travel Insurance (€30,000 minimum coverage)
  5. Flight Bookings (round-trip reservation)
  6. Hotel Bookings (entire trip duration)
  7. Bank Statements (last 3 months)
  8. Employment Letter (on company letterhead)
  9. Cover Letter (explaining trip purpose)
  10. Previous Visas (if applicable)

⏰ Processing Time

Standard Processing: 15-30 days from submission

Peak Season: May-August applications may take longer

Recommendation: Apply 4-6 weeks before travel

🗓️ Booking an Appointment

  1. Visit blsspain-uk.com
  2. Select "Edinburgh" as your centre
  3. Choose available date and time
  4. Fill in applicant details
  5. Receive confirmation email with reference number

Walk-ins are NOT accepted. Appointments are mandatory.

🔍 Tracking Your Application

Track your application online:

  1. Visit blsspain-uk.com
  2. Click "Track Application"
  3. Enter reference number and date of birth
  4. View current status

📦 Passport Collection

Two options available:

  • Self-Collection: Pick up from BLS Edinburgh (FREE)
  • Courier: Delivered to your address (additional fee)

Bring your original receipt and valid ID for collection.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does BLS Edinburgh accept cash payments?

No. BLS Edinburgh is 100% cashless. Only card payments accepted.

Can I submit documents for my family members?

Children under 12 don't need to appear personally. Adults and children 12+ must appear for biometrics (unless provided within 59 months).

What if my application is rejected?

You can reapply immediately with improved documentation. There's no waiting period.

Can I travel to other Schengen countries with Spain visa?

Yes, Spain visa allows travel across all 29 Schengen countries. Spain must be your main destination.

Is interview required at BLS Edinburgh?

Generally, no interview. You submit documents and biometrics. Embassy may call for interview in rare cases.
