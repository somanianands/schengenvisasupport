Frequently Asked Questions

Which BLS center should I apply from in UK?

Apply from the BLS center that serves your region. London serves South/East England and Wales, Manchester serves North England, Edinburgh serves Scotland. Check coverage above.

Can UK citizens apply for Spain visa via BLS?

UK citizens need visa for Spain/Schengen after Brexit. BLS handles applications for UK residents and nationals requiring Spain Schengen visa.

Does BLS UK accept cash?

NO. All BLS centers in UK are 100% cashless. Only debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) accepted. NO cash, NO cheques.

What are BLS UK operating hours?

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM (application submission). Saturday, Sunday, bank holidays: CLOSED. Arrive 15 minutes before appointment time.

How long does Spain visa take from UK?

Standard processing: 10-15 working days after Spanish consulate receives your documents from BLS. Total timeline including BLS processing: 2-3 weeks from appointment date.

Do I need Spain visa if I have UK visa/residence permit?

Yes. UK visa/residence permit doesn't grant entry to Schengen area. UK is not part of Schengen. You need separate Spain Schengen visa regardless of UK immigration status (unless you hold passport that's visa-exempt for Schengen).
