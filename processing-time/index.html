📆 What Counts as "Working Days"?

Processing time is counted in working days, NOT calendar days:

  • ❌ Weekends (Saturday, Sunday) don't count
  • ❌ Public holidays in the embassy's country don't count
  • ❌ Public holidays in your country don't count
  • ✅ Only Monday-Friday (non-holiday) days count
Example: If you submit on Monday and processing takes 15 working days, that's actually 3 weeks (21 calendar days), not 15 days!

⚠️ What Causes Delays?

  1. Incomplete Documents - Missing or incorrect documents
  2. Additional Verification - Embassy needs to verify information
  3. Peak Season - High volume of applications
  4. Interview Required - Called for additional questioning
  5. Travel History Check - Verifying previous visa records
  6. Embassy Holidays - Closures for national holidays
  7. First-Time Applicants - May require extra scrutiny

🚀 Tips to Avoid Delays

  1. Complete Documents - Submit ALL required documents first time
  2. Apply Early - 4-6 weeks before travel (6 weeks in peak season)
  3. Accurate Information - Double-check all details on application form
  4. Organize Documents - Present in logical order with index
  5. Clear Copies - High-quality photocopies, not blurry scans
  6. Correct Photos - Meet exact specifications (35×45mm)
  7. Valid Insurance - Covers entire trip + all Schengen countries
  8. Bank Statements - Recent (within 1-2 weeks of application)

❓ Can I Get Urgent/Express Processing?

❌ No Express Processing Available

Unlike some countries, there is no official express or urgent processing for Schengen visas. All applications go through standard processing. The only exception is medical emergencies or death in family (with proof), which may be expedited at embassy's discretion.

What to do if you need visa urgently:

  • Contact the embassy directly and explain your situation
  • Provide proof of emergency (medical documents, death certificate)
  • Be prepared that expediting is NOT guaranteed

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a Schengen visa?

Standard processing is 10-15 working days. During peak season (April-August), expect 15-30 days. In rare complex cases, it may take up to 45-60 days.

Can I get a Schengen visa in 3-5 days?

No. There is no express processing for Schengen visas. The minimum time is 10-15 working days. Always apply well in advance.

Why is my visa taking longer than 15 days?

Possible reasons: peak season, additional verification needed, incomplete documents, or embassy holidays. Track your application online and contact VFS if it exceeds 30 days.

How early can I apply for a Schengen visa?

You can apply up to 6 months before travel. However, apply no later than 15 days before departure (though this is cutting it too close).

Does VFS processing add extra time?

VFS adds 1-3 days for document transfer to/from embassy. This is included in the 15-day estimate. Count from your VFS appointment date.
