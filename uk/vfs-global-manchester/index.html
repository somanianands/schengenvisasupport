VFS Global Manchester: Complete Guide to Schengen Visa Application Center

Updated: February 12, 2026 · 9 min read

VFS Global Manchester serves applicants from North West England, North Wales, and surrounding areas for Schengen visa applications. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about the Manchester visa application center.

⚠️ Always Verify with Official Sources:

While we keep this guide updated, embassy policies and VFS procedures can change. Always check the official VFS Global website and your destination country's embassy website for the most current information, fees, and requirements before your appointment.

VFS Global Manchester Location

Important: The exact VFS Manchester address depends on which country you're applying to. Always check your appointment confirmation for the precise location.

💡 Pro Tip: After booking your appointment, you'll receive the specific address for your VFS center in Manchester. Different countries may use different locations.

🚇 Getting There:

  • By Tram: Manchester Metrolink serves most city center locations
  • By Train: Manchester Piccadilly and Manchester Victoria are main stations
  • By Bus: Multiple routes serve Manchester city center
  • By Car: Check appointment confirmation for nearest parking. NCP car parks available in city center
🅿️ Parking Tip: Manchester city centre parking can be expensive. Consider using park-and-ride or public transport. Typical parking costs £10-£20 for 2-3 hours.

Appointment Booking & Fees

How to Book

  1. Visit www.vfsglobal.com and select United Kingdom
  2. Choose your destination country
  3. Select Manchester as your preferred location (if available)
  4. Create account and complete visa application form
  5. Choose appointment date and time
  6. Pay VFS service fee online
  7. Receive confirmation with exact address
⚠️ Booking Advice: Manchester typically has better appointment availability than London. Book 2-3 weeks in advance during normal season, 4-6 weeks in summer (June-August).

Typical Fees (2026)

Service Fee (GBP)
Schengen Visa Fee £72 adults / £36 children (6-12)
VFS Service Fee £30-£50
Courier Return £15-£25
SMS Tracking £3-£5
Priority Service (if available) £40-£70 extra

What to Bring - Essential Documents

  • ✅ Printed appointment confirmation with address
  • ✅ Original passport (valid 3+ months)
  • ✅ Old passports (if any)
  • ✅ UK residence proof (BRP, visa, or UK citizenship evidence)
  • ✅ All required documents (originals + copies)
  • ✅ Valid debit/credit card for payment

Payment Methods - NO CASH

⚠️ CRITICAL - No Cash Accepted:

VFS Global Manchester DOES NOT ACCEPT CASH. All payments must be by debit or credit card only.

Accepted:

  • 💳 Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Maestro)
  • 💳 Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • ❌ Cash NOT accepted

What to Expect - Application Process

  1. Arrival (5 mins): Security check, present appointment confirmation
  2. Check-in (5-10 mins): Receive token number, wait for your turn
  3. Document Review (15-20 mins): Staff verify all documents
  4. Biometrics (5 mins): Fingerprints and photo
  5. Payment (5 mins): Pay by card at counter
  6. Receipt (2 mins): Get tracking number

Total time: 40-90 minutes depending on crowds

⏰ Best Times: Tuesday-Thursday mornings (10-11 AM) are usually quieter. Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons. Arrive 10-15 minutes before your slot.

After Submission - Tracking & Collection

Track Your Application

Use the VFS tracking portal with your reference number and passport number at www.vfsglobal.com

Processing Times

  • Standard processing: 10-15 working days (most countries)
  • Peak season (June-Aug): 15-20 working days
  • Priority service: 5-10 working days (if available)

Collection Options

Option 1: Collect in person at VFS Manchester (free)
Option 2: Courier delivery to your address (£15-£25)

Frequently Asked Questions - VFS Manchester

Where exactly is VFS Global Manchester located?

The exact address depends on your destination country. You'll receive the precise location in your appointment confirmation email after booking. Always check your confirmation before traveling.

Does VFS Manchester accept cash?

No, cash is NOT accepted. All payments must be made by debit or credit card. Ensure your card has sufficient funds (approximately £100-£120 total).

Can I apply in Manchester if I live outside North West England?

Yes, you can apply at any UK VFS center regardless of where you live in the UK. However, most applicants choose the nearest center for convenience.

Is parking available?

VFS centers typically don't have dedicated parking. Use nearby NCP car parks or public transport. Check your appointment confirmation for the nearest parking options. Public transport is recommended.

What if my country doesn't have VFS in Manchester?

Some countries only process applications in London or Edinburgh. Check the official VFS website for your destination country to see which centers are available.

Can someone else attend the appointment for me?

No, you must attend in person for biometrics. Exceptions: children under 12, applicants with biometrics from last 5 years, or medical exemptions (with certificate).

Do I need to live in Manchester to apply here?

No, you can apply at VFS Manchester from anywhere in the UK. You just need legal UK residence status (UK citizenship, BRP, valid visa, etc.).

What if I'm late for my appointment?

Late arrivals (15+ minutes) may be turned away and need to rebook. If running late, call VFS immediately. It's better to arrive 10-15 minutes early.

What are the visa fees at VFS Manchester?

Standard Schengen visa: £72 for adults (18+), £36 for children (6-17), free for under 6. VFS service fee: £30-£50 depending on country. Optional services available (premium lounge, photos, courier).

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

Book 2-3 months ahead, especially for summer travel (April-August). Check daily for cancellations if fully booked. Winter months typically have better availability.

What documents do I need for VFS Manchester?

Required: Valid UK passport (or foreign passport with UK visa/BRP), completed application form, 2 recent passport photos, travel insurance (£30,000+ coverage), flight/hotel bookings, bank statements (3 months), employment/school letter, and appointment confirmation.

How long does visa processing take from Manchester?

Standard processing: 10-15 working days after embassy receives your documents. Summer peak season may extend to 15-20 days. VFS forwards applications to embassy within 1-2 days. Total: 2-4 weeks from appointment.

How do I track my application after submission?

Use your VFS reference number to track on the country-specific portal. You'll receive email/SMS updates. Choose passport pickup at VFS or courier delivery when applying.
