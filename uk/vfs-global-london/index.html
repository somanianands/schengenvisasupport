VFS Global London: Complete Guide to Schengen Visa Application Centers

Updated: February 12, 2026 · 10 min read

VFS Global operates multiple visa application centers across London for Schengen visa applications. This comprehensive guide covers locations, appointment booking, hours, what to bring, and what to expect during your visit.

⚠️ Always Verify with Official Sources:

While we keep this guide updated, embassy policies and VFS procedures can change. Always check the official VFS Global website and your destination country's embassy website for the most current information, fees, and requirements before your appointment.

VFS Global London Locations

Important: VFS Global operates multiple centers in London. The specific location depends on which country you're applying to. Check your appointment confirmation for the exact address.

💡 Pro Tip: Different Schengen countries use different VFS centers in London. After booking your appointment, you'll receive the specific address. Don't assume all appointments are at the same location!

How to Book an Appointment

  1. Visit VFS Website: Go to www.vfsglobal.com and select United Kingdom, then your destination country
  2. Create Account: Register with your email and create a password
  3. Fill Application Form: Complete the online visa application form
  4. Choose Location & Time: Select London center and available time slot
  5. Pay Appointment Fee: Pay the VFS service fee (separate from visa fee)
  6. Receive Confirmation: Get appointment confirmation email with reference number and exact location address
⚠️ Important: Appointments fill up quickly, especially during summer (June-August) and holiday seasons. Book at least 3-4 weeks in advance. Tuesday-Thursday mornings typically have more availability than Mondays or Fridays.

What to Bring to Your Appointment

  • ✅ All required documents (originals + photocopies)
  • ✅ Valid debit/credit card for payment
  • ✅ Completed visa application form

Payment Methods - NO CASH ACCEPTED

⚠️ CRITICAL - Payment Policy 2026:

VFS Global UK and embassies DO NOT ACCEPT CASH PAYMENTS for visa fees. Only card payments are accepted.

Accepted Payment Methods:

  • 💳 Debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Maestro)
  • 💳 Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • ❌ Cash NOT accepted - Card payment mandatory
💡 Pro Tip: Bring both originals and photocopies of all documents. VFS staff verify originals and keep copies. Ensure your card has sufficient funds (approximately £100-£130 total) and international payments enabled.

Items NOT Allowed Inside VFS

What to Expect During Your Visit

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Security Check (5 minutes):

    Enter building and go through security screening. Have your appointment confirmation ready.

  2. Check-In (5-10 minutes):

    Show appointment confirmation at reception. Receive token number and wait in designated area.

  3. Document Review (15-20 minutes):

    VFS staff checks all your documents for completeness. They'll point out any missing items.

  4. Biometrics Collection (5 minutes):

    Fingerprints scanned digitally. Photo taken if required.

  5. Payment (5 minutes):

    Pay visa fee and VFS service charges by card at payment counter.

  6. Receipt & Tracking (2 minutes):

    Receive payment receipt and tracking number. Keep this safe!

Total Time: Expect 45-90 minutes depending on crowd levels.

⚠️ Arrival Time: Arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment. Late arrivals (15+ minutes) may not be accommodated and you'll need to rebook.

Biometrics Requirements

All first-time Schengen visa applicants must provide biometrics (fingerprints and photo). This data is stored in the VIS (Visa Information System) for 5 years.

Who Is Exempt?

  • Children under 12 years old
  • Applicants who provided biometrics in the last 5 years
  • People physically unable to provide fingerprints (medical certificate required)

Frequently Asked Questions - VFS London

Which VFS location in London will my appointment be at?

The specific location depends on your destination country and appointment availability. You'll receive the exact address in your appointment confirmation email. Common locations include Golden Cross House (Charing Cross) and Elsley House (Oxford Street area). Always check your confirmation for the precise address.

Does VFS London accept cash payments?

No, cash is NOT accepted. All payments including visa fees and VFS service charges must be made by debit or credit card only. Ensure your card has sufficient funds and international payments enabled.

Can I walk in without an appointment?

No, VFS London operates strictly by appointment only. You must book online in advance. Walk-ins are not accepted under any circumstances.

Do I need a UK residence permit to apply?

If you're not a UK citizen, you typically need proof of legal UK residence (BRP card, valid UK visa, settled status, etc.). Requirements vary by embassy - check your destination country's specific rules. Some countries allow tourist visa holders in UK to apply, others don't.

Can someone else submit my application?

No, you must attend in person for biometrics (fingerprints and photo). Exceptions: children under 12, applicants with previous biometrics (within 5 years), or medical exemptions with certificate.

How early should I arrive?

Arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled time. Do not arrive more than 30 minutes early as you may have to wait outside. Late arrivals (15+ minutes) risk being turned away and having to rebook.

What if I miss my appointment?

You'll need to rebook a new appointment online. Visa fees are typically non-refundable. Try to reschedule at least 24 hours in advance if you know you can't make it.

Is VFS Global the embassy?

No, VFS is an external service provider that collects documents for embassies. Your visa decision is made by the embassy/consulate, not VFS. VFS cannot advise on visa eligibility or decisions.

Can I apply for multiple Schengen countries at once?

No, each application must be submitted separately. If visiting multiple countries, apply to where you'll spend the most nights. If equal nights, apply to your first entry point.

Is there parking at VFS centers?

Most London VFS centers don't have dedicated parking. Use public transport (recommended) or nearby NCP car parks. Check your appointment confirmation for specific location and transport details.

What if my visa is rejected?

Visa fees are non-refundable. You'll receive your passport with a rejection letter explaining reasons. You can reapply with improved documentation but must pay full fees again.

Can I expedite my application?

Some embassies offer priority/express service through VFS for an additional fee (£40-£70). Not all countries offer this. Check when booking your appointment if urgent processing is available.
