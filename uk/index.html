How to Apply for Schengen Visa in UK

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Determine Your Destination Country:

    Apply to the country where you'll spend the most nights. If equal nights, apply to your first entry point.

  2. Check Eligibility:

    You typically need UK residence status (UK citizen, BRP holder, valid UK visa, settled/pre-settled status).

  3. Book Appointment:

    Visit www.vfsglobal.com, select UK and your destination country, create account, and book appointment at your preferred center.

  4. Prepare Documents:

    Gather all required documents: passport, UK residence proof, travel insurance, accommodation, flights, bank statements, etc.

  5. Attend Appointment:

    Visit VFS center with all documents. Provide biometrics (fingerprints and photo) and pay fees by card.

  6. Track Application:

    Use VFS tracking portal with your reference number to monitor progress.

  7. Collect Passport:

    Pick up in person or receive by courier once processed.

Frequently Asked Questions - UK VFS Centers

Can I apply at any UK VFS center?

Yes, you can apply at any VFS center in the UK regardless of where you live. Choose the most convenient location for you.

Do I need UK residence to apply?

Generally yes. You need to be legally resident in the UK (UK citizen, BRP holder, valid visa holder, settled/pre-settled status). Tourist visitors to UK typically cannot apply for Schengen visas in the UK.

Can I pay with cash at VFS UK?

No. All UK VFS centers only accept debit/credit card payments. Cash is not accepted.

Which UK VFS center is best?

London has most appointment availability but can be busiest. Manchester and Edinburgh often have shorter wait times and are less crowded. Choose based on your location and appointment availability.

How far in advance should I book?

Book 2-3 weeks ahead during off-season, 4-6 weeks ahead in summer (June-August). Some countries fill up faster than others.

Can someone else attend the appointment for me?

No, personal attendance is mandatory for biometrics (fingerprints and photo). Exceptions: children under 12, applicants with previous biometrics within 5 years, or medical exemptions with certificate.

What if my country doesn't use VFS?

Some countries (like Switzerland in some locations) have their own embassy visa sections. Check the embassy website directly if VFS doesn't list your destination country.

Is VFS Global the embassy?

No, VFS is a private company that collects visa applications on behalf of embassies. Your visa decision is made by the embassy/consulate, not VFS. VFS cannot advise on visa eligibility or decisions.
