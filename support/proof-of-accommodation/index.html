Proof of Accommodation for Schengen Visa

Proof of accommodation is mandatory for Schengen visa applications. You must show where you'll stay for EVERY night of your trip. This guide covers all accepted formats, how to get free cancellation bookings, and what to avoid.

Accepted Proof of Accommodation

  • ✅ Hotel bookings (most common)
  • ✅ Airbnb reservations
  • ✅ Hostel bookings
  • ✅ Invitation letter from host (with additional documents)
  • ✅ Rental agreements (long-term stays)

Hotel Bookings - Best Practice

Use Booking.com with FREE CANCELLATION - this is the safest option:

  1. Search hotels on Booking.com
  2. Filter: "Free cancellation" + "No prepayment"
  3. Book hotels for all nights
  4. Print confirmation emails
  5. Submit with visa application
  6. If visa rejected → Cancel for free
  7. If visa approved → Keep or modify bookings
⚠️ Never buy non-refundable hotels before visa approval! Many applicants lose hundreds of euros on rejected visas.

Staying with Family/Friends

If staying with someone, you need:

  • Invitation letter from host
  • Host's ID/residence permit copy
  • Host's proof of accommodation (rental contract or property deed)
  • Official sponsorship declaration (country-specific):
    • France: Attestation d'accueil (from town hall)
    • Germany: Verpflichtungserklärung (from Ausländerbehörde)
    • Spain: Carta de Invitación (from police station)

Common Mistakes

  • ❌ Not showing accommodation for all nights
  • ❌ Buying non-refundable bookings too early
  • ❌ Using fake booking confirmations (leads to visa ban)
  • ❌ Booking dates not matching flight dates
  • ❌ Missing host documents for private stays

FAQ

Can I use Airbnb for proof of accommodation?

Yes! Airbnb confirmations are accepted. However, Airbnb charges immediately, so it's riskier than Booking.com free cancellation hotels. Only use Airbnb if you're confident about visa approval.

Do I need to show accommodation for entire trip or just main cities?

ENTIRE trip. Every single night must be accounted for. If visiting 5 cities over 15 days, show hotel bookings for all 15 nights across all 5 cities.

What if I'm changing plans after visa approval?

That's fine! Use free cancellation bookings. After visa approval, you can cancel and book different hotels. The visa allows you to travel in Schengen - you don't need to follow the exact itinerary.
