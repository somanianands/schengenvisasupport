Common Questions

Can I use fake hotel bookings?

Don't. Embassies verify bookings directly with hotels. If they catch you, automatic rejection + possible ban. Use real bookings with free cancellation instead - same result, zero risk.

What if I'm staying with friends/family?

You'll need an invitation letter from them + proof they can host you (their lease/property docs) + their ID copy. Check our accommodation guide.

Do I need travel insurance if I have health insurance?

Yes - your regular health insurance probably doesn't cover you in Europe. You need specific Schengen travel insurance with €30,000 medical coverage + repatriation.

When should I cancel my hotel bookings?

After you get your visa! Once approved, you can change your plans if needed. If rejected, cancel within the free cancellation window (usually up to 24-48 hours before check-in).

How much does all this cost?

Done right: €90 visa fee + €30-80 insurance + €0 for hotels/flights (free cancellation/reservations). Total: ~€120-170. Doing it wrong can cost €1000+ if you buy tickets and get rejected.
