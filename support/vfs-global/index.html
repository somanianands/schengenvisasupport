VFS Global for Schengen Visa 2026: Complete Guide

Updated: February 1, 2026 · 14 min read

VFS Global is the outsourced visa application center used by many Schengen countries to process visa applications. This guide covers how to book appointments on VFS Global, what to expect at your appointment, service fees (€25-€35), optional services, document submission, biometrics collection, application tracking, and passport collection.

What Is VFS Global?

VFS Global is a private company that manages visa application processes on behalf of embassies and consulates. They handle:

  • ✅ Appointment scheduling
  • ✅ Document collection and verification
  • ✅ Biometric data collection (fingerprints, photo)
  • ✅ Application forwarding to embassy
  • ✅ Passport return
Important: VFS Global does NOT make visa decisions. They only collect your application and submit it to the embassy. The embassy makes the approve/reject decision.

Which Schengen Countries Use VFS Global?

VFS Global processes visas for these Schengen countries (varies by location):

  • 🇫🇷 France - Most locations worldwide
  • 🇩🇪 Germany - Selected countries
  • 🇮🇹 Italy - Many locations
  • 🇪🇸 Spain - Selected locations
  • 🇳🇱 Netherlands - Some locations
  • 🇵🇹 Portugal
  • 🇧🇪 Belgium
  • 🇸🇪 Sweden
  • 🇩🇰 Denmark
  • 🇫🇮 Finland
  • 🇳🇴 Norway

Check your specific country: Visit www.vfsglobal.com → Select your country → Check which embassies use VFS in your location.

💡 Note: Some countries use different service providers (TLS Contact, BLS International) or embassy direct applications. Always verify on the specific embassy website.

VFS Global Fees

Visa Fee (Paid to Embassy)

  • Adults: €90
  • Children 6-11: €45
  • Children under 6: Free

This is the embassy fee, NOT VFS fee. Paid at VFS but goes to embassy.

VFS Service Fee (Mandatory)

  • €25-€35 typically (varies by country and location)
  • Covers document processing, appointment booking, passport courier
  • Mandatory - cannot avoid this fee

Total Mandatory Cost

Adult applicant: €90 (visa) + €30 (VFS service) = €120 average

Optional Services (Can Skip These)
SMS Updates €3-€5 ✅ YES - Know when passport is ready
Courier Return €20-€30 ✅ YES - Save trip to collect passport
Photo Service €8-€10 ❌ NO - Bring your own photos (cheaper)
Photocopy Service €10-€20 ❌ NO - Make copies beforehand (€0.10/page)
Form Filling Assistance €15-€25 ❌ NO - Fill form yourself (free)
Prime Time Appointment €30-€50 ⚠️ MAYBE - Only if urgent and no regular slots
💡 Money-Saving Tip:
  • Skip photo service (bring photos: €5-€10 elsewhere)
  • Skip photocopy service (copy at local shop: €2-€5 total)
  • Skip form assistance (fill online yourself)
  • Get SMS updates (worth €3)
  • Get courier return if you live far (worth €20-€30)
Savings: €30-€50 per application

How to Book VFS Global Appointment

Step 1: Find Your VFS Center

  1. Go to www.vfsglobal.com
  2. Select your country of residence
  3. Select destination country (France, Germany, etc.)
  4. Click "Book an Appointment" or "Apply for Visa"

Step 2: Create Account

  • Register with email and password
  • Verify email
  • Log in to VFS portal

Step 3: Fill Application Form

  • Complete online visa application form
  • Save progress as you go
  • Takes 20-30 minutes
  • Print completed form (bring to appointment)

Step 4: Select Appointment Slot

  • Choose date and time (based on availability)
  • Availability: Can book 1-30 days in advance typically
  • Busy seasons: June-August appointments fill fast (book early!)
  • Off-season: November-March more availability

Step 5: Pay Appointment Fee (If Required)

  • Some VFS centers require small booking fee (€5-€10)
  • Others are free to book (pay at appointment)
  • Credit/debit card payment online

Step 6: Receive Confirmation

  • Email with appointment details
  • Print appointment confirmation letter
  • Note: Date, time, VFS center address
⚠️ Book Early: During peak season (May-July), VFS appointments can be fully booked 2-4 weeks out. Book as soon as you have your documents ready.

What to Bring to VFS Appointment

Required Documents (Mandatory)

  1. Printed appointment confirmation from VFS website
  2. Valid passport (original + copy of data page)
  3. Old passports (if you have travel history)
  4. Completed visa application form (printed and signed)
  5. Passport photos (2 recent biometric photos)
  6. Supporting documents:
    • Flight reservations
    • Hotel bookings
    • Travel insurance certificate
    • Bank statements (6 months)
    • Employment letter
    • Cover letter
    • Travel itinerary
    • Any additional documents for your visa type

Organization Tips

  • ✅ Make copies: Bring originals + 1 photocopy set of EVERYTHING
  • ✅ Use folders: Organize documents in order (checklist → passport → forms → supporting docs)
  • ✅ No staples: Use paper clips instead (VFS will separate documents)
  • ✅ Payment: Bring cash, credit card, or both (check accepted payment methods)

What Happens at VFS Appointment

1. Arrival (Arrive 15 Minutes Early)

  • Show appointment confirmation at entrance
  • Security check (no large bags, no weapons)
  • Get token number and wait

2. Document Verification (15-30 minutes)

  • VFS staff reviews your documents
  • Checks completeness (all pages, signatures, etc.)
  • May ask basic questions ("What's the purpose of your trip?")
  • Not an interview - just document check

3. Biometrics Collection (5 minutes)

  • Fingerprints: All 10 fingers scanned digitally
  • Photo: Digital photograph taken
  • Exemptions: Children under 12, people with finger injuries

Note: Biometrics valid 5 years. If you applied for Schengen visa in last 5 years, may not need new biometrics.

4. Fee Payment (5 minutes)

  • Pay visa fee (€90) + VFS service fee (€25-€35)
  • Select optional services (SMS, courier, etc.)
  • Get receipt

5. Receive Tracking Number

  • VFS gives you tracking/reference number
  • Use this to check application status online
  • Keep receipt safe

Total Time: 30-60 minutes

💡 VFS Appointment Tips:
  • Arrive 15 minutes early (not too early; they won't let you in more than 30 min before slot)
  • Dress neatly (not required, but looks professional)
  • Be polite to staff (they're just document processors, not decision-makers)
  • Double-check documents before leaving (staff may miss something)

After VFS Appointment: Tracking Application

Online Tracking

  1. Go to VFS Global website
  2. Click "Track Application"
  3. Enter reference number + passport number OR last name
  4. View status

Status Meanings
Application Received VFS has your documents, not yet sent to embassy
Application Forwarded to Embassy Documents sent to embassy for processing
Under Process Embassy is reviewing your application
Decision Made Embassy made decision (approved or rejected)
Passport Dispatched Passport sent back to VFS for collection
Ready for Collection You can pick up passport at VFS

Processing Times

  • Standard: 10-15 days (from VFS appointment to decision)
  • Peak season: 20-30 days (June-August)
  • Additional documents requested: +5-10 days

Passport Collection

Option 1: Collect in Person (Free)

  • Wait for "Ready for Collection" status or SMS
  • Go to VFS center with:
    • Receipt from application
    • Government-issued ID
  • Collect passport at counter
  • Check visa sticker BEFORE leaving VFS (verify dates, name spelling)

Option 2: Courier Delivery (€20-€30)

  • Select at appointment or online after submission
  • Passport delivered to your address
  • Takes 2-5 days after decision
  • Requires signature on delivery
  • Tracked courier service

Option 3: Authorize Representative

  • Can't collect yourself? Send someone else
  • Provide:
    • Authorization letter (signed by you)
    • Copy of your ID
    • Representative's original ID
    • Application receipt
⚠️ Check Visa Immediately: At VFS or upon receiving passport, verify:
  • Your name is spelled correctly
  • Passport number is correct
  • Visa dates match your application
  • Number of entries (single/multiple)
If any errors, report IMMEDIATELY to VFS/embassy.

Common VFS Global Issues & Solutions

1. No Appointment Slots Available

Solution:

  • Check daily (new slots released regularly)
  • Try early morning or late afternoon slots (less popular)
  • Consider nearby VFS centers (if multiple in your city/region)
  • Premium appointment option (extra fee but guaranteed slot)

2. VFS Rejected Documents

Solution:

  • VFS will tell you what's missing
  • Fix documents and return same day if possible
  • May need to rebook appointment if major issues

3. Lost Receipt

Solution:

  • Contact VFS helpline with reference number (from email)
  • Bring ID to VFS center
  • They can look up application with passport number

4. Application Taking Too Long

Solution:

  • Check status online first
  • If over 15 days with no update, contact VFS helpline
  • VFS can escalate to embassy
  • Remember: VFS doesn't control processing speed (embassy does)

FAQ

Can I apply without VFS if embassy directly accepts applications?

Depends on the country. Some embassies ONLY use VFS (France in many locations). Others offer both options. Check specific embassy website for your location. VFS is often more convenient (more locations, easier appointments).

Will VFS staff interview me?

NO. VFS does NOT conduct visa interviews. They only:

  • Verify documents are complete
  • Collect biometrics
  • Forward application to embassy
The embassy may request an interview separately (rare for tourist visas, more common for work/study visas).

Can VFS guarantee visa approval?

NO. VFS is an application collection center, NOT decision-maker. Only the embassy approves or rejects visas. VFS cannot influence decisions or "speed up" approvals.

What if I make a mistake on the application form?

Minor errors: Cross out neatly, write correction, initial next to it. Major errors: Print new form and refill. VFS won't accept messy/illegible forms.

Can I reschedule my VFS appointment?

YES. Log in to VFS portal and reschedule (usually free, up to 24 hours before appointment). After that, may need to book new appointment.

Do I need to submit my passport at VFS?

YES. You must submit original passport. It will be with embassy/VFS for 10-20 days. Cannot travel during this time. Plan accordingly.
