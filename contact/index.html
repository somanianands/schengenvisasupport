Frequently Asked Questions

Before sending your message, check if your question is answered here:

Can you review my documents and tell me if my visa will be approved?

No. We are not visa consultants or immigration lawyers. We cannot review individual cases or predict approval chances. Our guides provide general information only. For personalized advice, consult a licensed immigration professional.

I'm having trouble with my specific application. Can you help?

We cannot assist with individual applications. If you have questions about your specific case:

  • Contact the embassy directly (they're the decision-makers)
  • Contact VFS Global if you used their services
  • Consult a visa consultant or immigration lawyer

Where can I find information about [specific country] visa?

Check our Countries section for available guides. If we don't have a guide for your destination yet, check the official embassy website for that country in your location.

The information on your site contradicts what I found elsewhere. Which is correct?

Visa requirements can vary by:

  • Your country of residence
  • Specific embassy location
  • Recent regulation changes
Always verify critical information on the official embassy website for your specific situation. If you found outdated information on our site, please report it using the contact form so we can update.

How long will it take to get a response?

We aim to respond within 3-5 business days. During peak seasons (May-July), responses may take up to 7 days. Please be patient!

Can you expedite my visa processing?

No. We have no relationship with embassies and cannot influence processing times. Only the embassy controls your application timeline.

Where to Find Help for Your Application

If you need help with your specific visa application:

🏛️ Embassy/Consulate

The official source for requirements and your application status. Find contact information on their official website.

📞 VFS Global Helpline

If you applied through VFS Global, their customer service can help with appointment issues, document questions, and tracking. Find their helpline on www.vfsglobal.com

👨‍💼 Visa Consultants

Licensed professionals who can review your documents, provide personalized advice, and help with complex cases. Search for "visa consultant [your city]" or "immigration lawyer near me"

💬 Online Communities

  • Reddit: r/travel, r/immigration
  • Travel forums: TripAdvisor, Lonely Planet
  • Reddit communities for your destination country (r/travel, r/solotravel)

Fellow travelers share experiences, but take advice with caution - always verify with official sources.
