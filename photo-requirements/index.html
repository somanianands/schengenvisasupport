📸 Schengen Visa Photo Requirements 2026

Your visa photo is one of the first things officials see. A wrong photo can delay your application! This guide covers the exact specifications required for Schengen visa photos.

📏 Official Photo Specifications

📐
Size
35mm × 45mm
⬜
Background
White or Light Gray
👤
Face Height
32-36mm (70-80%)
📅
Recency
Within 6 months

Complete Technical Specifications:

Photo Size35mm wide × 45mm tall
Background ColorWhite, off-white, or light gray (uniform, no patterns)
Face CoverageFace should cover 70-80% of photo height (32-36mm)
Head PositionCentered, facing forward, looking at camera
ExpressionNeutral expression, mouth closed
EyesOpen, clearly visible, looking at camera
GlassesNOT allowed (even prescription glasses)
Head CoveringsOnly for religious reasons (face must be fully visible)
Photo RecencyTaken within last 6 months
Photo QualityHigh quality print, no pixelation, good lighting
Quantity2 identical photos required

✅ Do's and ❌ Don'ts

✅ DO

  • Use white or light gray background
  • Look directly at camera
  • Keep neutral expression
  • Ensure face is evenly lit
  • Keep eyes open and visible
  • Have clear, sharp focus
  • Print on high-quality photo paper
  • Remove glasses completely

❌ DON'T

  • Use colored backgrounds
  • Wear sunglasses or glasses
  • Smile with teeth showing
  • Have shadows on face
  • Wear hats or caps
  • Use old photos (over 6 months)
  • Use selfies
  • Print on regular paper

🚫 Common Photo Mistakes That Get Rejected

  1. Wrong Size - Not exactly 35×45mm
  2. Colored Background - Blue, red, or patterned backgrounds
  3. Wearing Glasses - Even prescription glasses are NOT allowed
  4. Smiling - Teeth visible or wide smile
  5. Old Photo - Looking different from current appearance
  6. Poor Lighting - Shadows on face, overexposed, too dark
  7. Face Too Small - Face doesn't cover 70-80% of photo
  8. Not Centered - Face off to one side
  9. Blurry/Pixelated - Low resolution print
  10. Red Eye - Flash reflection in eyes

👥 Special Cases

Religious Head Coverings

  • Allowed for religious reasons (hijab, turban, etc.)
  • Face must be fully visible from chin to forehead
  • No shadows from head covering on face

Babies and Children

  • Same size requirements apply (35×45mm)
  • Eyes should be open and looking at camera
  • No toys, pacifiers, or parents visible
  • White background required
  • For infants: slightly relaxed rules on expression

Medical Conditions

  • Eye patches allowed with medical certificate
  • Hearing aids are acceptable
  • If you can't open eyes due to medical condition, include documentation

📍 Where to Get Visa Photos

Best Options:

  1. Professional Photo Studios - Ask specifically for "Schengen visa photos"
  2. VFS/Embassy Centers - Many have photo booths on-site (₹300-500)
  3. Passport Photo Shops - Usually near embassies/VFS centers

Tips When Getting Photos:

  • Tell them it's for a "Schengen visa" - they know the specs
  • Ask for 4-6 copies (extra for your records)
  • Check photos before leaving - verify size and background
  • Cost: ₹100-300 for 6 photos typically
⚠️ Avoid: Printing at home on regular paper, using phone selfies, or getting photos from automated kiosks that don't meet specs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear glasses in my visa photo?

No. Since 2017, glasses are NOT allowed in Schengen visa photos - even prescription glasses. Remove them before taking the photo.

Can I smile in my visa photo?

A slight, natural smile with mouth closed is acceptable. But teeth should NOT be visible and expression should remain neutral.

Is blue background acceptable?

No. Only white, off-white, or light gray backgrounds are accepted for Schengen visa photos.

How recent should my photo be?

Within the last 6 months. If you've changed appearance significantly (weight, hair, beard), take new photos.

Can I use my passport photo?

Only if it meets Schengen specs (35×45mm, white background) AND is less than 6 months old. Many passport photos are different sizes.

How many photos do I need?

2 identical photos are required. One goes on the application form, one for the visa sticker. Get 4-6 copies for safety.

📸 Quick Checklist Before Submitting

  • ✅ No glasses
  • ✅ Face covers 70-80% of photo
  • ✅ Neutral expression, mouth closed
  • ✅ Taken within last 6 months
  • ✅ High quality print, no pixelation
