⚡ Urgent Schengen Visa

Need a Schengen visa urgently? While there's no official "express visa," there are ways to speed up the process. Here's what you can do.

Last updated: March 2026. We review this page against official Schengen/EU visa policy pages and relevant embassy or VFS instructions.

The Reality Check

Honest Truth: Schengen visas normally take 10-15 working days. There's no guaranteed "same day" or "48-hour" visa. But you can maximize your chances of faster processing.

When Embassies MAY Fast-Track

  • 🏥 Medical emergency - Urgent treatment abroad
  • ⚰️ Death in family - Funeral of close relative in Europe
  • 💼 Urgent business - Critical meeting, contract signing
  • 🎓 Academic urgency - Exams, thesis defense
  • ⚖️ Court appearance - Legal proceedings

Note: You must provide proof of the emergency!

How to Request Urgent Processing

  1. Contact embassy directly - Explain your situation
  2. Write "URGENT" cover letter - State reason for urgency
  3. Attach proof - Hospital letter, death certificate, etc.
  4. Request priority appointment - Some VFS centers offer this
  5. Be flexible - Take any available appointment slot

VFS Prime Time Appointments

Many VFS centers offer Prime Time Service:

  • Early morning or late evening appointments
  • Costs extra (around ₹2000-4000)
  • Doesn't speed up processing, just appointment availability
  • Useful when regular slots are fully booked

Tips for Faster Processing

1. Perfect Documentation

  • Complete application = faster processing
  • Missing documents cause delays
  • Use checklist, double-check everything

2. Choose Less Busy Embassy

  • Some embassies process faster
  • Apply to country with shorter wait times
  • Smaller countries often faster than France/Germany

3. Avoid Peak Seasons

  • Summer (May-July) is slowest
  • December holiday rush
  • Apply in off-season when possible

4. Apply Early

  • You can apply up to 6 months before travel
  • Earlier = more time for processing
  • Don't wait until last minute!

What NOT to Do

  • ❌ Don't book non-refundable flights before visa
  • ❌ Don't fake emergencies - Fraud is detected and banned
  • ❌ Don't use "visa agents" promising guaranteed fast visa
  • ❌ Don't call embassy daily - It doesn't speed things up
  • ❌ Don't pay bribes - Legitimate process only

Emergency Appointment Checklist

If requesting urgent appointment, prepare:

  • ✅ Letter explaining emergency with dates
  • ✅ Proof of emergency (hospital letter, death certificate, business letter)
  • ✅ Complete visa application ready to submit
  • ✅ All supporting documents organized
  • ✅ Travel insurance already purchased
  • ✅ Flight booking (refundable recommended)

Realistic Timeline

Standard application10-15 working days
Peak season15-30 working days
Genuine emergency (approved)3-5 working days
Missing documents+10-15 days extra

Alternative: Apply to Different Country

If one country has no appointments:

  • Check if another Schengen country has availability
  • Adjust your itinerary to enter that country first
  • Or spend most days in that country
  • Valid Schengen visa works for all 29 countries
