VFS vs TLScontact for Schengen Visa

Updated: March 2026 · 7 min read · Reviewed by Schengen Visa Support Editorial Team

Applicants often ask whether VFS Global or TLScontact is better. In practice, your provider is usually determined by the embassy and your residence location. This guide compares process, fees, and tracking so you know what to expect.

Official-source note: Always book only through the official VFS/TLS country page linked by your embassy.

VFS vs TLScontact: Key Differences

Point VFS TLScontact
Coverage Wider global footprint for many Schengen missions Strong in selected markets and missions
Booking flow Account + appointment slot + checklist Account + appointment slot + checklist
Service fees Varies by country/mission Varies by country/mission
Passport tracking Usually via reference + surname Usually via account or tracking page

What Actually Matters More Than Provider

  • Correct embassy jurisdiction selection
  • Complete and consistent documents
  • Clear travel purpose and funding evidence
  • Early appointment booking during peak season

Common Mistakes

  • Booking on unofficial third-party websites
  • Confusing visa fee with service fee
  • Assuming appointment provider decides approval

How to Choose Correctly

  1. Check embassy website for official appointment partner.
  2. Use only official provider links.
  3. Confirm jurisdiction and center location before paying any fee.

FAQs

Does VFS or TLScontact influence approval chance?

No. Decision is made by the consulate, not the appointment center.

Can I switch from VFS to TLS for the same embassy?

Usually no. You must use the provider designated for your jurisdiction.
