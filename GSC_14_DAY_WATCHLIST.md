2. Set Date to Last 28 days, then Compare with previous period.
3. Filter by each query below and check the mapped page.
4. Track CTR movement first, then clicks, then average position.

## What changed on-page

- Updated query-matched title and meta descriptions for VFS city pages (Chennai, Ahmedabad, Hyderabad).
- Updated query-matched title and meta descriptions for BLS city pages (Mumbai, New Delhi, Bangalore).

## Success criteria by Day 14

- At least 6 of 10 queries show CTR increase vs baseline.
- At least 4 of the 0-click queries register first clicks.
- No major average position drop (>2.0) on the improved pages.

## If CTR does not improve by Day 14

- Expand title variants with stronger intent words: fees, booking, appointment, address, latest updates.
- Add 2-3 lines of concise above-the-fold summary matching query wording exactly.
- Add FAQ question phrasing matching real query syntax for the affected page.
