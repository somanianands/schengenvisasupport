How to Apply for Spain Visa at BLS

  1. Register Online: Create account at blsspain-india.com
  2. Fill Application: Complete Spain Schengen visa application form
  3. Book Appointment: Select preferred BLS center and date
  4. Prepare Documents: Gather all required documents (passport, photos, travel insurance, etc.)
  5. Pay Fees: Pay online during booking
  6. Visit BLS Center: Attend appointment with all documents
  7. Biometrics: Provide fingerprints and photo
  8. Track Application: Monitor status on BLS portal
  9. Collect Passport: Pick up from BLS or receive via courier

Required Documents

  • Valid Indian passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Completed Spain visa application form
  • 2 recent passport-size photographs (35mm x 45mm, white background)
  • Travel insurance (minimum €30,000 coverage)
  • Flight reservations (confirmed bookings)
  • Hotel reservations for entire stay in Spain
  • Bank statements for last 6 months
  • Income Tax Returns (ITR) for last 2 years
  • Employment letter or business documents
  • Cover letter explaining purpose of visit
  • Schengen travel itinerary

Processing Time

Standard Processing: 10-15 working days from the date of submission at BLS

Peak Season: 15-20 working days (April-August, December)

BLS Submission to Embassy: 1-2 working days

Total Timeline: Plan for at least 3-4 weeks before your travel date

Key Information

  • ✅ Book appointments 2-3 months in advance during peak season
  • ✅ Arrive 15 minutes before your appointment time
  • ✅ Bring original documents + 1 complete photocopy set
  • ✅ Personal attendance mandatory for biometrics (few exceptions)
  • ✅ Check center-specific requirements on appointment confirmation
  • ⛔ Cash NOT accepted - only card/online payments
  • ⛔ Walk-ins not entertained - online booking required
