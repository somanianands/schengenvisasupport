Frequently Asked Questions - VFS Hyderabad

Where exactly is VFS Global Hyderabad located?

VFS Global Hyderabad is in the HITEC City/Gachibowli area, Telangana's IT hub. Exact address provided in your appointment confirmation based on destination country. Near Botanical Garden Metro Station.

Does VFS Hyderabad accept cash payments?

NO. VFS Global Hyderabad is cashless. Only debit/credit cards, online payments, or demand drafts accepted. Do not bring cash.

What are the visa fees at VFS Hyderabad?

Schengen visa: ₹7,100 adults (18+), ₹3,550 children (6-17), free under 6. VFS service fee: ₹2,000-₹3,000 by country. Optional: courier (₹500-₹800), premium lounge (₹3,500+).

What are VFS Hyderabad operating hours?

Application submission: Mon-Fri 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM, Sat 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM. Passport collection: Mon-Fri 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM. Closed Sundays and holidays.

Which regions does VFS Hyderabad serve?

VFS Hyderabad serves Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. This includes Hyderabad, Secunderabad, Warangal, Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, Tirupati, and all of both states.

How do I reach VFS Hyderabad by Metro?

Take Hyderabad Metro Red Line to Botanical Garden or Durgam Cheruvu stations. VFS in HITEC City area is 10-15 minute auto ride from Metro (₹50-₹100). Ola/Uber easily available from Metro stations.

Is parking available at VFS Hyderabad?

Paid parking available at HITEC City towers (₹50-₹100 for 2-3 hours). HITEC City has good road access. TSRTC buses serve HITEC City from all parts of Hyderabad.

How far in advance should I book?

Book 2-3 months ahead, especially during peak IT employee travel season (May-August) and holiday seasons. Check daily for cancellations. Winter months have better availability.

Can I submit for multiple Schengen countries?

No, each appointment is country-specific. Apply to main destination (most nights). Separate appointments required for different countries.

What documents do I need?

Valid passport (6+ months), application form, 2 photos (35mm x 45mm), travel insurance (€30,000+), flight/hotel bookings, bank statements (6 months), employment/business documents, ITR, appointment confirmation.

Are walk-ins accepted?

No, walk-ins NOT accepted. Must book online via country-specific VFS portal. No same-day appointments.

Can someone attend on my behalf?

Generally NO. Personal attendance required for biometrics. Exceptions: Children under 12, previous biometrics within 59 months.

How long does processing take?

Standard: 10-15 working days after embassy receives documents. Peak season may be 15-20 days. VFS forwards within 1-2 days. Total: 2-4 weeks.

How do I track my application?

Use VFS reference number on country-specific portal. Email/SMS updates provided. Return: VFS pickup (bring slip + ID) or courier delivery.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, log into VFS account to reschedule (if available). Must reschedule 2+ days before. Multiple reschedules may delay processing.
