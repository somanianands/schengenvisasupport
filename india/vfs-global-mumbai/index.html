Frequently Asked Questions - VFS Mumbai

Where exactly is VFS Global Mumbai - BKC center?

VFS Global Mumbai main center is located in Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC), near Kurla. Additional centers operate in Andheri and Lower Parel. Check your appointment confirmation for the exact address based on your destination country.

Does VFS Mumbai accept cash payments?

NO. VFS Global Mumbai operates cashless. Only debit cards, credit cards, online payments, or demand drafts are accepted. Do not bring cash to your appointment.

What are the visa fees at VFS Mumbai?

Standard Schengen visa: ₹7,100 for adults (18+), ₹3,550 for children (6-17), free for under 6. VFS service fee: ₹2,000-₹3,000 depending on country. Optional: courier return (₹500-₹800), premium lounge (₹3,500+).

What are VFS Mumbai operating hours?

Application submission: Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM. Passport collection: Monday-Friday 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM. Closed on Sundays and public holidays (Indian and embassy holidays).

Which regions does VFS Mumbai serve?

VFS Mumbai serves Maharashtra (except Pune), Goa, Southern Gujarat, Dadra & Nagar Haveli, and Daman & Diu. Pune has its own dedicated VFS center.

How do I reach VFS Mumbai BKC by local train?

Take Central or Harbour line to Kurla Station, then auto/taxi (10 min, ₹50-₹100) to BKC. Or take Western line to Bandra, then auto to BKC (15 min, ₹100-₹150). Metro Line 1 connects to Western line stations.

Is parking available at VFS Mumbai?

Limited paid parking available at BKC center (₹50-₹100 for 2-3 hours). Public transport strongly recommended due to BKC traffic congestion. Multiple BEST bus routes serve BKC.

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

Book 2-3 months ahead, especially during peak season (April-August, December). Check daily for cancellations if fully booked. Winter months (January-March, September-November) typically have better availability.

Can I submit applications for multiple Schengen countries?

No, each appointment is country-specific. Apply to the country where you'll spend the most nights (main destination). Separate appointments required for different destination countries.

What documents do I need for VFS Mumbai appointment?

Required: Valid Indian passport (minimum 6 months validity), completed application form, 2 recent passport photos (35mm x 45mm, white background), travel insurance (€30,000+ coverage), flight reservations, hotel bookings, bank statements (last 6 months), employment/business documents, ITR, and appointment confirmation.

Does VFS Mumbai accept walk-in appointments?

No, walk-ins are NOT accepted. You must book your appointment online in advance through the country-specific VFS portal. Same-day appointments are not available.

Can someone else submit my application at VFS Mumbai?

Generally NO. Applicants must appear in person for biometric data collection (fingerprints and photo). Exceptions: Children under 12 and applicants who have provided biometrics in last 59 months.

How long does visa processing take from VFS Mumbai?

Standard processing: 10-15 working days after embassy receives documents. Peak season (summer, Diwali, Christmas) may take 15-20 days. VFS forwards applications within 1-2 days. Total timeline: 2-4 weeks from appointment.

How do I track my passport after submitting at VFS Mumbai?

Use your VFS reference number to track on the country-specific portal. You'll receive email/SMS updates at each stage. Passport return: Pick up at VFS (bring collection slip + ID) or courier delivery to your address (if selected and paid).

Can I reschedule my VFS Mumbai appointment?

Yes, log into your VFS account on the country-specific portal to reschedule (subject to availability). Must reschedule at least 2 days before appointment. Frequent rescheduling may affect your application processing.
