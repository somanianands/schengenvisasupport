Frequently Asked Questions - VFS Bangalore

Where exactly is VFS Global Bangalore located?

VFS Global Bangalore is located at Prestige Trade Tower in the MG Road/Brigade Road area, near Trinity Metro Station. Check your appointment confirmation for the exact floor and office number based on your destination country.

Does VFS Bangalore accept cash payments?

NO. VFS Global Bangalore is completely cashless. Only debit/credit cards, online payments, or demand drafts are accepted. Do not bring cash to your appointment.

What are the visa fees at VFS Bangalore?

Standard Schengen visa: ₹7,100 for adults (18+), ₹3,550 for children (6-17), free for under 6. VFS service fee: ₹2,000-₹3,000 depending on country. Optional services: courier return (₹500-₹800), premium lounge (₹3,500+), photo service.

What are VFS Bangalore operating hours?

Application submission: Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM. Passport collection: Monday-Friday 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM. Closed Sundays and public holidays (Indian and embassy holidays).

Which regions does VFS Bangalore serve?

VFS Bangalore serves Karnataka and northern Kerala. This includes Bangalore, Mysore, Mangalore, Hubli-Dharwad, Belgaum, Calicut, Kannur, and surrounding areas.

How do I reach VFS Bangalore by Metro?

Take Namma Metro Purple Line or Green Line to MG Road Metro Station. VFS is at Prestige Trade Tower, a 5-minute walk from the station. Exit toward MG Road/Brigade Road and follow signs to Prestige Trade Tower.

Is parking available at VFS Bangalore?

Limited paid parking available at Prestige Trade Tower (₹50-₹100 for 2-3 hours). MG Road area has heavy traffic. Metro strongly recommended. Multiple BMTC bus routes serve MG Road if coming from different parts of Bangalore.

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

Book 2-3 months ahead, especially during peak season (April-June, December). Check daily for cancellations. Off-peak months (January-March, September-November) have better availability. IT city schedules fill quickly.

Can I apply for multiple Schengen countries at once?

No, each appointment is for one country only. Apply to the country where you'll spend the most nights (main destination rule). You need separate appointments for different destination countries.

What documents are required for VFS Bangalore?

Valid Indian passport (6+ months validity), completed application form, 2 passport photos (35mm x 45mm, white background), travel insurance (€30,000+ coverage), flight reservations, hotel bookings, bank statements (6 months), employment/business documents, ITR, appointment confirmation.

Are walk-in appointments accepted at VFS Bangalore?

No, walk-ins NOT accepted. You must book online in advance through the country-specific VFS portal. Same-day appointments not available.

Can someone else submit my application?

Generally NO. Personal attendance mandatory for biometric collection (fingerprints + photo). Exceptions: Children under 12 and applicants who provided biometrics within last 59 months may have representatives.

How long does processing take from VFS Bangalore?

Standard: 10-15 working days after embassy receives documents. Peak season may extend to 15-20 days. VFS forwards within 1-2 days. Total timeline: 2-4 weeks from appointment date.

How do I track my passport?

Use your VFS reference number on the country-specific tracking portal. Email/SMS updates sent at each stage. Passport return: Pick up at VFS (bring collection slip + valid ID) or courier delivery (if selected and paid).

Can I reschedule my VFS Bangalore appointment?

Yes, log into VFS account on country-specific portal to reschedule (subject to availability). Must reschedule at least 2 days before appointment. Multiple reschedules may delay processing.
