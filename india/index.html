VFS Global Centers in India

VFS Global operates multiple visa application centers across India for Schengen visa applications. With centers in major cities, applicants can access visa services conveniently across the country.

🌐 All Centers Process 23 Schengen Countries

All VFS Global centers in India process visa applications for Austria, Belgium, Croatia, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, and Switzerland.

Note: France uses TLS Contact (different service provider).

💳 Payment Methods

NO CASH ACCEPTED at any VFS center. Payment by:

  • Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
  • Online payment during booking
  • Demand Draft (specific centers only)

How to Apply

  1. Choose Your Center: Select the VFS center that serves your region
  2. Select Country: Choose your Schengen destination from 23 available countries
  3. Create Account: Register on VFS Global portal with valid email
  4. Complete Application: Fill online application form accurately
  5. Book Appointment: Select date and time based on availability
  6. Pay Fees: Complete online payment (card/net banking)
  7. Prepare Documents: Gather all required documents before appointment
  8. Visit Center: Arrive 15 minutes early with documents and passport
  9. Biometrics: Submit fingerprints and photo at center
  10. Track Application: Monitor progress via VFS portal or SMS

General Operating Hours

Application Submission:

  • Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM (select centers only)
  • Sunday: Closed

Passport Collection:

  • Monday - Friday: 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Or opt for courier delivery (additional fee)

Closed on: All Indian national holidays, embassy holidays (may vary by country)

Important Information

📱 Required Items

  • Valid passport (min 6 months validity)
  • Appointment confirmation
  • Completed application form
  • All required documents
  • Payment receipts

⏰ Processing Time

  • Standard: 15-20 working days
  • Peak season: Up to 30 days
  • Apply 3-4 weeks before travel
  • Can apply 6 months in advance

📸 Biometrics

  • Required for all applicants
  • Valid for 5 years
  • Children over 12 years included
  • Must be present in person

📞 Contact Support

  • VFS Call Center: Check city page
  • Email: Available on VFS portal
  • Peak hours: Avoid 10 AM - 2 PM
  • Track online to reduce calls
