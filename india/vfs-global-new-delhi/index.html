VFS Global New Delhi - Schengen Visa Center

⚠️ Always Verify with Official Sources

Information on this page is provided for guidance only. VFS Global schedules, fees, and procedures can change. Always verify current details on the official VFS Global website and your destination country's embassy before visiting.

Location & Contact

📍 Address: Multiple VFS centers across Delhi NCR

Main Center: Shivaji Stadium, Connaught Place area

📞 Call Center: +91-11-4242-8747 / +91-11-4242-8748

🌐 Website: visa.vfsglobal.com/ind/en/

📧 Email: Available through VFS portal

🚇 How to Reach

  • Delhi Metro: Shivaji Stadium Metro Station (Orange Line - Airport Express), 5-minute walk
  • Other Stations: Pragati Maidan, Barakhamba Road (Blue Line)
  • Bus: Multiple DTC routes to Connaught Place
  • Parking: Limited street parking, use public transport recommended
  • Auto/Cab: Widely available, use app-based services

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the operating hours of VFS Global New Delhi?

Application submission: Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM. Passport collection: Monday-Friday 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How much are the visa fees at VFS New Delhi?

Adult visa fee: ₹7,100, Child visa fee (6-17 years): ₹3,550, VFS service fee: ₹2,000-₹3,000 depending on country and service type.

Does VFS Global New Delhi accept cash payments?

NO. VFS Global New Delhi does NOT accept cash. Payment must be made by debit/credit card, online payment, or demand draft only.

Which Schengen countries are processed at VFS New Delhi?

VFS New Delhi processes 23 Schengen countries: Austria, Belgium, Croatia, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, and Switzerland.

How do I book an appointment at VFS New Delhi?

Visit visa.vfsglobal.com/ind/en/, select your destination country, create an account, fill the application form, select appointment date, and pay online.

Can I walk in without an appointment?

No. All appointments must be booked online in advance. Walk-ins are not accepted at VFS New Delhi.

How long does visa processing take from New Delhi?

Standard processing takes 15-20 working days. During peak season, it may extend to 30 days. Apply at least 3-4 weeks before your intended travel date.

What documents do I need to bring?

Valid passport (6 months validity), appointment confirmation, completed visa application form, photographs, travel insurance, flight bookings, hotel reservations, bank statements, and any country-specific documents.

Is biometric required at VFS New Delhi?

Yes, biometric (fingerprints and photo) is required for all applicants over 12 years. Biometric data is valid for 5 years across all Schengen countries.

Can someone else collect my passport?

Yes, with authorization letter, photocopy of your ID, original ID of the collecting person, and collection slip.

What if my visa is rejected from New Delhi?

You can reapply with stronger documentation or appeal the decision through the embassy. VFS Global is only the collection center; visa decisions are made by embassies.

Is parking available at VFS New Delhi?

Limited street parking is available. We strongly recommend using Delhi Metro or app-based cab services for convenience.

Can I track my application online?

Yes, use the tracking number provided at submission. Visit VFS portal and enter your reference number and passport number to check status.

What is Premium Lounge service?

Premium Lounge offers priority processing, dedicated counter, comfortable waiting area, and faster service. Fee: ₹3,500+. Book during appointment scheduling.

Do children need to attend in person?

Yes, all applicants including children above 12 years must attend for biometric collection. Children under 12 should attend but biometric not required.
