Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a Poland appointment from India?

Use the official VFS login page for this route and complete booking in your selected city center.

Where do I find latest address and center timings?

Use the official Attend-Centre link for your city. It shows latest address, timings, and submission process.

Should I buy final flight tickets before visa approval?

Usually no. Use reservation/itinerary first and buy non-refundable tickets only after approval.
