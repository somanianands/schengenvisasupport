Frequently Asked Questions - VFS Chennai

Where exactly is VFS Global Chennai located?

VFS Global Chennai is in the Ashok Nagar/Nungambakkam area. Exact address provided in your appointment confirmation based on destination country. Near Ashok Nagar Metro Station (Green Line).

Does VFS Chennai accept cash payments?

NO. VFS Global Chennai operates cashless. Only debit/credit cards, online payments, or demand drafts accepted. Do not bring cash.

What are the visa fees at VFS Chennai?

Schengen visa: ₹7,100 adults (18+), ₹3,550 children (6-17), free under 6. VFS service fee: ₹2,000-₹3,000 by country. Optional: courier (₹500-₹800), premium lounge (₹3,500+), photo service.

What are VFS Chennai operating hours?

Application submission: Mon-Fri 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM, Sat 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM. Passport collection: Mon-Fri 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM. Closed Sundays and public holidays.

Which regions does VFS Chennai serve?

VFS Chennai serves Tamil Nadu, Puducherry, and Andaman & Nicobar Islands. This includes Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Tiruchirappalli, Salem, Tirunelveli, and all of Tamil Nadu.

How do I reach VFS Chennai by Metro?

Take Chennai Metro Green Line to Ashok Nagar Metro Station. VFS is approximately 10-minute walk from the station. Auto-rickshaws readily available from Metro station for short distance (₹30-₹50).

Is parking available at VFS Chennai?

Limited paid parking available near the center (₹50-₹100 for 2-3 hours). Public transport recommended. Multiple MTC bus routes serve Ashok Nagar/Nungambakkam. Metro + auto is convenient option.

How far in advance should I book?

Book 2-3 months ahead, especially for summer travel (April-August). Check daily for cancellations if fully booked. Winter months (November-February) typically have better availability.

Can I submit for multiple Schengen countries?

No, each appointment is country-specific. Apply to your main destination (country with most nights). Separate appointments needed for different countries.

What documents do I need?

Valid passport (6+ months validity), application form, 2 passport photos (35mm x 45mm), travel insurance (€30,000+), flight/hotel bookings, bank statements (6 months), employment/business proof, ITR, appointment confirmation.

Are walk-ins accepted?

No, walk-ins NOT accepted. Must book online through country-specific VFS portal in advance. No same-day appointments.

Can someone attend on my behalf?

Generally NO. Personal attendance required for biometrics. Exceptions: Children under 12, applicants with biometrics from last 59 months.

How long does processing take?

Standard: 10-15 working days after embassy receives documents. Peak season may be 15-20 days. VFS forwards within 1-2 days. Total: 2-4 weeks.

How do I track my application?

Track using VFS reference number on country portal. Email/SMS updates provided. Passport return: VFS pickup (bring collection slip + ID) or courier delivery.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, log into VFS account to reschedule (if available). Must reschedule at least 2 days before. Multiple reschedules may impact processing.
