VFS Global Washington DC - Schengen Visa Application Center

VFS Global Washington DC is the primary visa application center for Schengen visas in the Mid-Atlantic United States, serving residents of DC, Maryland, Virginia, West Virginia, Delaware, and the Carolinas.

⚠️ Always Verify with Official Sources:

VFS Global procedures, fees, and appointment availability can change. Always check the official VFS Global portal and embassy website for your destination country before applying.

VFS Global Washington DC
1325 G Street NW, Suite 1025
Washington, DC 20005
United States

📞 Phone: +1-202-507-0600 (VFS Helpline - varies by country)

🌐 Website: www.vfsglobal.com

🗺️ Location: Downtown DC, near Metro Center, between 13th and 14th Streets NW

💡 Getting There: Metro: Red/Blue/Orange/Silver lines to Metro Center. Exit 13th/G Street NW. Walk west 2 blocks. Building on north side of G Street.

Operating Hours

Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Saturday - Sunday: Closed
Holidays: Closed on US federal holidays

⏰ Arrive Early:

Arrive 15 minutes before appointment. Security screening required for building entry. Late arrivals may not be accommodated.

Fees & Payment

⚠️ NO CASH ACCEPTED:

VFS Global Washington DC operates CASHLESS. Only credit or debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) are accepted.

Standard Fees (USD)

  • Visa Fee: $90 for adults (ages 12+)
  • Visa Fee: $45 for children (ages 6-11)
  • VFS Service Fee: $25-$50 (varies by country)
  • Optional Services: Premium lounge, courier return, photo service

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where is VFS Global Washington DC located?

1325 G Street NW, Suite 1025, Washington, DC 20005, in Downtown DC near Metro Center. Accessible via Red/Blue/Orange/Silver lines to Metro Center station.

2. Does VFS Washington DC accept cash?

NO. VFS Global Washington DC operates cashless. Only credit or debit cards are accepted.

3. Which states does VFS Washington DC serve?

Washington DC, Maryland, Virginia, West Virginia, Delaware, North Carolina, and South Carolina (Mid-Atlantic region).

4. How do I reach VFS Washington DC by Metro?

Take Metro Red, Blue, Orange, or Silver line to Metro Center station. Exit at 13th/G Street NW. Walk 2 blocks west on G Street to 1325 G Street NW.

5. What are VFS Washington DC operating hours?

Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Closed weekends and US federal holidays.

6. What are the visa fees at VFS Washington DC?

Standard visa fee: $90 for adults (12+), $45 for children (6-11). VFS service fee varies by country ($25-$50). Optional: Premium lounge ($45-$75), photo service ($15), courier return ($25-$35).

7. Is parking available near VFS Washington DC?

Yes, nearby parking garages include PMI Parking at 1200 G Street NW and Colonial Parking. Rates $15-$30 per 2-3 hours. Metro is highly recommended to avoid downtown parking costs.

8. How far in advance should I book my appointment?

Book 2-3 months ahead, especially April-August (peak season). Check daily for cancellations if fully booked. Winter (November-February) has better availability.

9. Can I submit applications for multiple Schengen countries?

No, each appointment is country-specific. Apply to the country where you'll spend the most nights. Separate appointments required for different destinations.

10. What documents do I need for my appointment?

Required: Valid passport (or foreign passport with US visa/green card), application form, 2 passport-size photos, travel insurance (€30,000+ coverage), flight reservations, hotel bookings, bank statements (last 3 months), employment/school letter, and appointment confirmation.

11. Does VFS DC offer same-day or walk-in appointments?

No, walk-ins are not accepted. You must book online in advance through the country-specific VFS portal. For urgent travel, some embassies offer expedited processing (additional fees apply).

12. Can someone submit my application on my behalf?

Generally NO. Personal attendance is mandatory for biometric collection (fingerprints and photo). Exceptions: Children under 12 and elderly/disabled with medical proof may have representatives.

13. How long does visa processing take from VFS DC?

Standard: 10-15 business days after embassy receives documents. Peak season may extend to 15-20 days. VFS forwards to embassy within 1-2 days. Total timeline: 2-4 weeks from appointment.

14. Can I track my passport after submission?

Yes, use your VFS reference number on the country-specific portal. Email/SMS notifications provided. Passport return options: Pick up at VFS or courier delivery (if selected and paid).

15. What if I need to reschedule my VFS DC appointment?

Log into your VFS account on the country-specific portal to reschedule (subject to availability). Most centers allow rescheduling up to 2 days before. Frequent rescheduling may impact your application.
