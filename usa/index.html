Common Questions About VFS USA

Do I need to travel to a VFS center?

Yes, you must appear in person to submit biometrics (fingerprints and photo). Choose the center nearest to your location from the list above.

Can I use any VFS center in the USA?

Yes, you can visit any VFS center regardless of your state of residence. However, choosing the nearest center is most convenient.

How far in advance should I book?

Book 2-3 months before travel. Summer (May-August) and holidays are peak seasons with limited appointments.

What if there are no appointments?

Check multiple times daily - slots open up due to cancellations. Consider alternative VFS centers or expedited services if urgent.

Can I track my passport?

Yes, VFS provides tracking through their portal using your reference number. Most processing takes 10-15 business days.
