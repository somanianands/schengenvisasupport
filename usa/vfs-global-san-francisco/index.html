VFS Global San Francisco - Schengen Visa Application Center

VFS Global San Francisco serves Northern California, Nevada, Oregon, Washington State, and the entire Western US for Schengen visa applications.

VFS Global procedures and fees can change. Always check the official VFS Global portal and embassy website before applying.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Which states does VFS San Francisco serve?

Northern California, Nevada, Oregon, Washington State, Idaho, Montana, Wyoming, Alaska, and Hawaii.

2. Does VFS SF accept cash?

NO. Card payments only (Visa, Mastercard, American Express).

3. How do I book an appointment?

Visit country-specific VFS portal → create account → select San Francisco center → choose date → pay online.

4. How long does processing take?

10-15 business days typically. Peak season (summer) may be 15-20 days.

5. Can Portland/Seattle residents use this center?

Yes! San Francisco serves all of Oregon and Washington State.

6. Where exactly is VFS San Francisco located?

VFS Global San Francisco is at 2 Embarcadero Center, Suite 1900, San Francisco, CA 94111. Near Embarcadero BART station, accessible via Muni lines.

7. What are VFS San Francisco operating hours?

Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Closed weekends and US federal holidays. Appointment times vary throughout the day.

8. What are the visa fees?

Standard visa: $90 adults (12+), $45 children (6-11). VFS service fee: $25-$50 depending on country. Optional: Premium lounge ($45-$75), photos ($15), courier return ($25-$35).

9. How do I reach VFS by BART?

Take any BART line to Embarcadero station. Exit at Embarcadero Center. VFS is in Embarcadero Center Tower 2, 19th floor. 2-minute walk from station.

10. Is parking available?

Embarcadero Center has paid parking garages ($3-$7/hour). Nearby options: Golden Gateway Garage, 101 California Garage. BART/Muni strongly recommended to avoid SF parking costs.

11. What documents are required?

Valid passport (or foreign passport with US visa/green card), completed application, 2 photos, travel insurance (€30,000+ coverage), flight/hotel reservations, bank statements (3 months), employment letter, appointment confirmation.

12. Can I walk in without an appointment?

No, appointments are mandatory. Book online through your destination country's VFS portal. Same-day appointments not available.

13. How long does processing take?

Standard: 10-15 business days. Peak summer season (June-August) may be 15-20 days. VFS submits to embassy within 1-2 days. Total: 2-4 weeks.

14. How do I track my application?

Use your VFS reference number on the country-specific tracking portal. You'll receive email/SMS updates. Choose pickup at VFS or courier delivery when applying.

15. Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, log into your VFS account to reschedule (subject to availability). Must reschedule at least 2 days before appointment. Frequent changes may affect processing.
