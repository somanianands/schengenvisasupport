Frequently Asked Questions

1. Which states does VFS Houston serve?

Texas, Louisiana, Oklahoma, Arkansas, Mississippi, Alabama, Tennessee, Kentucky, Florida, and Georgia (entire South/Southeast).

2. Does VFS Houston accept cash?

NO. Card payments only (Visa, Mastercard, American Express).

3. Can Dallas/Austin residents use this center?

Yes! VFS Houston serves all Texas residents including Dallas, Austin, San Antonio.

4. Can Miami/Atlanta residents use this center?

Yes! VFS Houston serves Florida and Georgia. It's the only VFS center in the Southeast region.

5. Is parking free?

Building parking typically has hourly fees ($2-$5/hour). Nearby Galleria mall has paid parking options.

6. Where exactly is VFS Houston located?

VFS Global Houston is at 5444 Westheimer Road, Suite 1750, Houston, TX 77056 in the Galleria area, near Uptown Houston.

7. What are VFS Houston operating hours?

Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Closed on weekends and US federal holidays.

8. What are the visa fees in Houston?

Standard visa: $90 adults (12+), $45 children (6-11). VFS service fee: $25-$50 by country. Optional services: Premium lounge ($45-$75), photo service ($15), courier return ($25-$35).

9. How do I get there by Metro?

Houston Metro Red Line to Wheeler → transfer to bus route 82 (Westheimer). Limited Metro coverage in Galleria area. Driving, Uber, or Lyft recommended for all areas of Houston.

10. What documents do I need?

Valid passport (or foreign passport with US visa/green card), completed application, 2 passport photos, travel insurance (€30,000+ coverage), flight/hotel reservations, bank statements (3 months), employment/school letter, appointment confirmation.

11. How far in advance should I book?

Book 2-3 months ahead, especially in summer (May-August). Winter (November-February) typically has better availability. Check for cancellations daily if booked out.

12. Are walk-in appointments available?

No, you must book online through the country-specific VFS portal in advance. Same-day and walk-in applications are not accepted.

13. Can someone submit my application for me?

No, personal attendance is required for biometric data collection (fingerprints and photo). Exceptions: Children under 12, elderly/disabled with medical documentation.

14. How long does processing take from VFS Houston?

Standard: 10-15 business days after embassy receives documents. Peak season may be 15-20 days. VFS forwards to embassy within 1-2 days. Total timeline: 2-4 weeks from appointment date.

15. Can I track my application and reschedule?

Track using your VFS reference number on the country-specific portal (email/SMS updates provided). To reschedule: log into VFS account at least 2 days before your appointment.
