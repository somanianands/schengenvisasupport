How to Find Your VFS Global Center

VFS Global Main Site: www.vfsglobal.com

To find your VFS center:

  1. Visit the embassy website of your destination country
  2. Look for "Visa Application" or "VFS Global" section
  3. Select your country/location to find the nearest VFS center
  4. Book appointment through the country-specific VFS portal

Other Visa Service Providers

Some countries use alternative providers:

  • TLS Contact: www.tlscontact.com (France, Turkey in some countries)
  • BLS International: www.blsinternational.com (India, several EU countries)
