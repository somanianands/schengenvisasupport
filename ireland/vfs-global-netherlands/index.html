VFS Global Netherlands Dublin: Complete Guide (2026)

If you are applying for a Netherlands Schengen visa from Ireland, use the official VFS Netherlands portal and Dublin attend-centre page below for appointment booking and latest center details.

🔗 Official Netherlands VFS Links:
  • Login / Appointment: https://visa.vfsglobal.com/irl/en/nld/login
  • Attend-Centre (Dublin): https://visa.vfsglobal.com/irl/en/nld/attend-centre/Dublin

Tip: Use the attend-centre page for latest address, hours, and service updates before visiting.

Appointment Process (Netherlands)

  1. Go to the Netherlands VFS login page and create/sign in to your account.
  2. Complete your visa application details and upload supporting information if requested.
  3. Select Dublin as the center (when available).
  4. Choose appointment slot and pay VFS service charges.
  5. Attend appointment with complete document set and biometric submission.

Documents Checklist (Typical)

  • Passport: Valid passport + previous passports (if applicable) (passport rules)
  • Application form: Complete and signed form for your visa type (form-filling guide)
  • Photos: Recent compliant photos (photo requirements)
  • Travel insurance: Schengen-compliant coverage (insurance guide)
  • Flight reservation: Round-trip reservation/itinerary (flight itinerary guide)
  • Accommodation proof: Hotel booking or host documents (hotel booking guide)
  • Financial proof: Recent bank statements and balance evidence (bank statement guide)
  • Purpose proof: Cover letter + day-by-day trip plan (cover letter · travel itinerary)
  • Master list: Full checklist by visa category (required documents)
⚠️ Important: Requirements may change by profile and travel purpose. Always confirm final checklist on the official VFS and Netherlands embassy pages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this page only for Netherlands applications from Ireland?

Yes. This guide is only for applicants using the Ireland route to the Netherlands via VFS Dublin.

Where do I check the latest center address and timings?

Always use the official Dublin attend-centre page: Netherlands attend-centre Dublin.

Can I start my appointment booking from this guide?

Yes. Use the official Netherlands VFS login page: VFS Netherlands Ireland login.

Should I buy non-refundable flight tickets before visa approval?

No. Use a reservation/itinerary first, then buy final tickets after approval. See flight reservation support.

How much bank balance is needed for a Netherlands Schengen visa?

There is no one-size-fits-all amount for every applicant. Show stable, sufficient funds that cover your full trip and regular expenses. See bank statement requirements and bank balance guidance.

How long does Netherlands visa processing take from Ireland?

Timelines vary by season and individual case. Apply early and check current estimates on official portals. You can review general trends in our processing time guide.

Can I track my application after submission?

Yes. Use your reference number on the relevant tracking portal after biometrics and document submission. See visa tracking guide.

What are the most common reasons for refusal?

Common issues include weak financial proof, unclear travel purpose, inconsistent documents, or insufficient ties. Review visa rejection guide and common mistakes before applying.

Do I need confirmed hotels for every night?

You should provide accommodation proof aligned with your itinerary. Many applicants use refundable bookings. See hotel booking guide.

Can freelancers and self-employed applicants apply through this route?

Yes. Freelancers and self-employed applicants can apply if they provide proper financial and professional documentation. See freelancer guide.
