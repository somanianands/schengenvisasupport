🇦🇪 Schengen Visa from Dubai/UAE

Living in the UAE and planning a Europe trip? Good news - you can apply for Schengen visa from Dubai without going back to your home country. Here's everything you need to know.

If you are searching for Schengen visa from UAE, Schengen visa UAE requirements, Schengen visa UAE documents, or Schengen visa UAE processing time, this guide is built for you.

Who Can Apply from UAE?

  • ✅ UAE residents with valid residence visa
  • ✅ Expats from India, Pakistan, Philippines, etc.
  • ✅ People working in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah
  • ✅ Valid Emirates ID holders
Important: Your UAE residence visa should be valid for at least 3 months beyond your return date from Europe.

Documents Required (UAE Applicants)

Standard Documents

  • ✅ Original passport (6+ months validity, 2 blank pages)
  • ✅ Completed visa application form
  • ✅ Passport photos (35×45mm, white background)
  • ✅ Travel insurance (€30,000 coverage)
  • ✅ Flight itinerary
  • ✅ Hotel booking
  • ✅ Travel itinerary

UAE Residence Proof (IMPORTANT)

  • ✅ Emirates ID - Front and back copy
  • ✅ UAE Residence Visa - Page from passport
  • ✅ Visa validity check - From GDRFA (ICA) website
  • ✅ NOC from employer - If employed in UAE

Financial Proof

  • ✅ UAE bank statements (3-6 months)
  • ✅ Salary certificate from UAE employer
  • ✅ Salary slips (last 3 months)
  • ✅ Employment contract (sometimes requested)

Special Tip for Indian Expats

As an Indian living in UAE, you have advantages:

  • ✅ Faster processing than from India
  • ✅ Stronger financial profile (UAE salary)
  • ✅ No need to travel back to India to apply
  • ✅ Weekend trips to Europe are easy
Pro Tip: UAE residence makes you look financially stable. Highlight your UAE employment and savings!

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Choose destination country - Apply to main destination or first entry
  2. Book VFS appointment online
  3. Gather all documents - See checklist above
  4. Fill application form - Online or at VFS
  5. Visit VFS center - Submit documents + biometrics
  6. Pay fees - Visa fee + VFS service charge
  7. Track application - Using reference number
  8. Collect passport - Or opt for courier delivery

Can Family Apply Together?

Yes! If your spouse and children have UAE residence visas, they can apply together:

  • Submit applications at the same time
  • Include marriage/birth certificates
  • One person can sponsor the whole family
  • Children under 12 pay reduced visa fee

FAQs

My UAE visa expires soon. Can I still apply?

Your UAE visa should be valid for 3+ months after your return from Europe. If not, renew it first.

Can I apply from UAE on a visit visa?

No. You need a valid UAE residence visa to apply from UAE. Visit visa holders should apply from their home country.

Which bank statements - UAE or home country?

Primarily UAE bank statements. You can add home country statements as additional support.
