We have special focus on Ireland to Schengen visas because:

  • Ireland is NOT in the Schengen Area (despite EU membership)
  • Irish residents need visas for continental Europe travel
  • Specific requirements for Irish bank statements, employment letters
  • VFS Global Dublin processes many Schengen applications
  • Confusion about whether Irish residence permits help

Our Ireland to Schengen section addresses these unique needs.
